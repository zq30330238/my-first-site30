Daz was a member of the 104th Training Corps who ranked near the bottom of his class. He was known for his cowardice and lack of confidence, needing constant encouragement from his peers to keep going...Daz was a member of the 104th Training Corps who ranked near the bottom of his class. He was known for his cowardice and lack of confidence, needing constant encouragement from his peers to keep going. During the Battle of Trost, Daz was one of the recruits who nearly gave up completely, but was motivated by Connie's persistence. He eventually joined the Survey Corps, though he never became a frontline fighter. Daz's character represented the ordinary human being caught in extraordinary circumstances — not everyone could be a hero, but everyone had to find a way to survive. He was caught in Zeke's Titan transformation during the Shiganshina battle and became a Pure Titan, an ironic end for someone who had always been afraid of becoming Titan food.
