Uryu Ishida

Quincy

Affiliation: Quincy, Karakura Town

Rank: Last Quincy

Overview

Uryu Ishida is the last surviving Quincy of the Ishida bloodline and a classmate of Ichigo. Initially antagonistic toward Soul Reapers due to historical Quincy persecution, he evolves into a trusted ally and friend. His pride and skill as a Quincy make him a formidable fighter.

Appearance

Uryu has short black hair and wears glasses, giving him a studious appearance. He typically wears the Karakura High School uniform but don his Quincy uniform — a white hakama-like outfit — in battle. He carries his Quincy bow, which manifests from reishi.

Personality

Uryu is proud, intelligent, and initially rigid in his beliefs. He maintains a cool and aloof demeanor but has a strong sense of justice and fairness. His rivalry with Ichigo pushes him to grow, and he eventually acknowledges Soul Reapers as worthy allies.

Abilities

Uryu is a master of Quincy techniques including Heilig Pfeil (sacred arrows) and Hirenkyaku (high-speed movement). He uses various Ginto (spirit tools) and has advanced techniques like Sprenger and Licht Regen. He develops the Vollstandig power later in the series.

Trivia

Uryu's grandfather Souken Ishida was killed by Mayuri Kurotsuchi, creating a deep hatred. His father Ryuken Ishida is a doctor and also a Quincy of immense power. Uryu's sense of fashion is particularly traditional for his age.
