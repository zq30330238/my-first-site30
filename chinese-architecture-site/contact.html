Contact

Contact Myers Architecture

We'd love to hear from you. Reach out with questions, suggestions, or collaboration ideas.

Thank you for your interest in Myers Architecture. We welcome your questions, comments, and suggestions. Whether you have a specific question about Chinese architecture, a suggestion for content you'd like to see, or an idea for collaboration, we would be delighted to hear from you. Our team endeavors to respond to all inquiries within two business days.

Get in Touch

You can reach us by email at contact@myersarchitecture.com. We particularly welcome inquiries related to: architectural research and collaboration, content suggestions and corrections, media and press inquiries, and feedback about our website. For the fastest response, please include a clear subject line that indicates the nature of your inquiry.

Follow Our Work

Stay connected with Myers Architecture for the latest content about Chinese architecture. We regularly update our site with new articles exploring different aspects of Chinese architectural heritage. Whether you are interested in the structural ingenuity of dougong brackets, the serene beauty of Suzhou gardens, or the dramatic silhouette of the Potala Palace, our growing collection of articles offers something for every architectural enthusiast.

"The best way to learn about architecture is to ask questions, explore buildings, and share what you discover. We welcome your questions and look forward to learning together."

— Myers Architecture Team

Location

Myers Architecture is based in the United States, with contributors and researchers located across Asia and North America. Our remote team allows us to bring diverse perspectives to our coverage of Chinese architecture while maintaining close connections with the places and traditions we write about.
