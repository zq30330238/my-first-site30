Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

This privacy policy describes how Myers Architecture collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide when using our website at chinese-architecture.jycsd.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information through Google Analytics, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages viewed. This information is used to analyze usage patterns, improve our content, and understand how visitors interact with our site. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it to us through our contact form or email.

We also use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings page. For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, visit www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device until they expire or are deleted). You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, but please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our site.

"Your privacy matters to us. We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve our service, and we never sell or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes."

— Myers Architecture Privacy Policy

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services on our website: Google Analytics for usage analysis, Google AdSense for advertising, and Google Fonts and Tailwind CSS CDN for visual presentation. These services may collect information about your visit to our site as described in their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers for complete information about their data handling practices.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information including the right to access, correct, or delete your data; the right to object to or restrict processing; the right to data portability; and the right to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights or to ask questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at contact@myersarchitecture.com.

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically. This policy was last updated on May 27, 2026.
