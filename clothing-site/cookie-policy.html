Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and deliver targeted advertisements.

2. How We Use Cookies

Myers Fashion uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic functionality of our website.
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site, which pages are most popular, and how users navigate the site.
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads based on your interests and browsing history.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings for future visits.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use services from third parties that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics — tracking and analytics
  • Google AdSense — behavioral advertising

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Check your browser's help section for instructions.
  • Opt-Out Platforms: You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
  • Ad Settings: Adjust your ad personalization settings at Google Ad Settings.

Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at contact@myersfashion.com.
