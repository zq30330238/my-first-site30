Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Myers Fashion ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website clothing.jycsd.com.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: Voluntarily provided information such as your name and email address when you contact us.
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected information including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience. See our Cookie Policy for details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Improve user experience through analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Serve relevant advertisements (Google AdSense)
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

  • Google Analytics — for website usage analytics
  • Google AdSense — for advertising
  • Cloudflare — for content delivery and security

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the use of your data.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Opt out of cookies and tracking
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@myersfashion.com.
