Fashion Week Trends

The world of fashion revolves around four major cities. Each season, designers from Paris, Milan, London, and New York unveil collections that set the global style agenda. Explore our coverage of runway trends, emerging designers, and the stories behind the collections.

"Fashion week is not just about clothes. It is a cultural barometer — a reflection of the zeitgeist captured in fabric, silhouette, and color twice a year."

The undisputed capital of haute couture. Paris Fashion Week showcases the most prestigious houses in fashion — Chanel, Dior, Louis Vuitton, Saint Laurent, and more.
