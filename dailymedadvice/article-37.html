• Common symptoms include persistent fatigue, pale skin, shortness of breath, brittle nails, and cold extremities.
  • Women, pregnant individuals, vegetarians, and people with digestive disorders are at highest risk.
  • Boost iron levels naturally by eating heme and non-heme iron sources, pairing them with vitamin C, and avoiding inhibitors like tea and calcium with meals.
  • If diet isn't enough, supplements or IV iron under medical supervision can restore levels quickly.
  • Always consult a healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and personalized treatment--don't self-diagnose.
