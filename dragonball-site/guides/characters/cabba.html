Cabba

Universe 6 Saiyan Protector

Category: Characters · Home

SpeciesSaiyan (Universe 6)
First AppearanceDragon Ball Super Episode 32 / Chapter 7 (2016)
Power LevelBase: ~8e10; Super Saiyan: ~4e12; SSJ2 (ToP): ~2e14. Rapidly growing through Vegeta's mentorship.
Signature MovesGalick Gun (taught by Vegeta), Final Flash (attempted), Ki Blast Volley, Super Saiyan (Universe 6 variant)

Cabba is a Saiyan from Universe 6's Planet Sadala, a world where the Saiyan race evolved along a fundamentally different path than their Universe 7 counterparts. Rather than becoming brutal planet conquerors, Universe 6's Saiyans became noble protectors who operated as a galactic peacekeeping force, wearing sleek blue-and-gold uniforms and defending the innocent.Cabba is a Saiyan from Universe 6's Planet Sadala, a world where the Saiyan race evolved along a fundamentally different path than their Universe 7 counterparts. Rather than becoming brutal planet conquerors, Universe 6's Saiyans became noble protectors who operated as a galactic peacekeeping force, wearing sleek blue-and-gold uniforms and defending the innocent. Cabba embodies this heroic Saiyan ideal: polite, soft-spoken, and genuinely compassionate, yet possessing the same innate battle lust and pride that defines Saiyans across all universes. His encounter with Vegeta during the Universe 6 Tournament was transformative for both characters. Vegeta, moved by the existence of a Saiyan civilization untainted by Frieza's genocide and the warrior culture's brutality, saw in Cabba a reflection of what the Saiyan race could have become. In turn, Vegeta's mentorship — and his brutal demonstration of Super Saiyan Blue by punching Cabba in the face — taught the young Saiyan how to access the Super Saiyan transformation through concentrated rage rather than anger at his own weakness. Cabba's subsequent achievement of Super Saiyan, his tears of joy and gratitude toward Vegeta, and his promise to grow stronger marked one of the franchise's most genuinely touching moments. During the Tournament of Power, Cabba fought valiantly as a member of Universe 6's team, achieving Super Saiyan 2 and demonstrating tactical growth. His bond with Caulifla, a fiery female Saiyan he personally mentored, and Kale, her shy protector, expanded Universe 6's Saiyan lore. When Universe 6 was erased by Zeno, Cabba's final moments were spent thanking Vegeta for everything — a testament to the profound mentor-student bond that transcended universal boundaries. Cabba represents the Saiyan potential that was never realized in Universe 7: strength used for protection rather than conquest. ... Read more
