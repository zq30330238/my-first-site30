Monaka

Beerus's Pretend Champion

Monaka is the diminutive, large-nippled delivery driver whom Beerus presented to Goku as the strongest warrior he had ever faced, a deception designed to motivate the Saiyan's training by giving him a seemingly unreachable goal. In reality, Monaka is a gentle, physically unimposing alien whose sole contribution to the Universe 6 Tournament was being assigned as the fifth member of Universe 7's team despite having absolutely no combat ability whatsoever.Monaka is the diminutive, large-nippled delivery driver whom Beerus presented to Goku as the strongest warrior he had ever faced, a deception designed to motivate the Saiyan's training by giving him a seemingly unreachable goal. In reality, Monaka is a gentle, physically unimposing alien whose sole contribution to the Universe 6 Tournament was being assigned as the fifth member of Universe 7's team despite having absolutely no combat ability whatsoever. Beerus's plan was to keep Monaka on the bench as a motivational carrot, then have him face a weakened final opponent who would ostensibly be defeated by Monaka's incredible secret power. The tournament's unexpected twists forced Beerus to send Monaka into the ring against Hit, the legendary assassin who had just dismantled Vegeta. In one of Dragon Ball Super's funniest sequences, Hit — having developed respect for Goku — threw the fight by dramatically collapsing from a single, comically weak Monaka punch, preserving the delivery driver's fraudulent reputation and giving Universe 7 the winning point. Goku, who had genuinely believed Monaka was the strongest being in existence, was inspired by the (fake) display of power to train even harder. Monaka's design is absurdist Toriyama at its finest: a small, pink, bug-eyed alien with grotesquely prominent nipples, whose day job involves delivering packages in a modest spaceship. His total lack of combat ability is contrasted with his sincere, hardworking personality as a humble wage earner who got roped into cosmic shenanigans. Monaka represents the joke that Dragon Ball has never stopped being a comedy: even amid gods, multiversal tournaments, and existence-threatening stakes, a delivery driver with big nipples can be the secret weapon that wins the day. Beerus continues to mention Monaka as a motivational tool, and Goku continues to believe in the mythical strength of his hero, making Monaka the most influential fraud in Universe 7's history. ...
