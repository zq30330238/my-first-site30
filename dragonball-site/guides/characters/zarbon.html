Zarbon is Frieza's sophisticated and ruthlessly loyal top advisor, a strikingly handsome alien nobleman who served as the tyrant's most trusted administrator and enforcer on Planet Namek. Unlike the brutish Dodoria, Zarbon cultivated an image of refined elegance, with emerald-green hair, polished armor, and a carefully maintained appearance.Zarbon is Frieza's sophisticated and ruthlessly loyal top advisor, a strikingly handsome alien nobleman who served as the tyrant's most trusted administrator and enforcer on Planet Namek. Unlike the brutish Dodoria, Zarbon cultivated an image of refined elegance, with emerald-green hair, polished armor, and a carefully maintained appearance. Yet beneath the beauty lay a monstrous secret: Zarbon possessed the ability to transform into a hideous reptilian beast whose power dwarfed his elegant base form. He abhorred this form, considering it grotesque and shameful, and only resorted to it when pushed to the absolute limit. This internal conflict between vanity and necessity defined Zarbon's combat style. On Namek, Zarbon first defeated Vegeta with relative ease, the Saiyan's 24,000 power level proving insufficient against Zarbon's base power of 23,000 plus the experience gap. However, when Vegeta returned after a near-death Zenkai boost that pushed him to 30,000, Zarbon was forced to transform into his hated reptilian form. The monster form's raw power allowed him to crush Vegeta a second time in a brutal display. Zarbon's fatal miscalculation came when he failed to verify Vegeta's death, allowing the Saiyan to recover and gain yet another near-death power boost. In their third and final encounter, Vegeta killed Zarbon with a single punch and an energy blast, the transformed advisor's final screams echoing across Namek's green skies. Zarbon's character, with his elegant facade hiding monstrous capability, and his genuine shame about his transformative power, added psychological depth to what could have been a simple henchman role. His dynamic with Frieza also hinted at the broader political structure of the Planet Trade Organization, where even the highest advisors lived in constant fear of their emperor. ... Read more
