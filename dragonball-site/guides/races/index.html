All Races & Organizations

16 race/organization guides

Saiyan Race

A warrior race from Planet Vegeta known for Zenkai boosts, Great Ape transformation, and Super Saiyan forms. Nearly extinct after Frieza destroyed their planet. Represented by Goku, Vegeta, Broly, Gohan, and Trunks. Saiyans grow stronger after near-death and thrive on battle.

View Details

Namekian Race

A peaceful slug-like species from Planet Namek capable of regeneration, limb stretching, and ki-based magic. Notable for creating Dragon Balls and possessing fusion abilities. Represented by Piccolo, Dende, Nail, and Kami. They sustain themselves on water alone and reproduce asexually through eggs.

View Details

Frieza Race

A mysterious mutated species producing some of the universe's strongest warriors, also called Frost Demons. Notable for multiple transformative forms that suppress true power. Represented by Frieza, King Cold, and Cooler. Their natural durability allows survival in the vacuum of space.

View Details

Majin Race

Primordial beings of pure evil created by the wizard Bibidi. Possess infinite regeneration, absorption abilities, and transmutation magic. Represented by Kid Buu, Fat Buu, Super Buu, and Uub (reincarnation). Their power scales based on whom or what they absorb into their malleable bodies.

View Details

Androids / Cyborgs

Artificially enhanced beings created by Dr. Gero and the Red Ribbon Army. Range from fully mechanical (Android 16) to human-based cyborgs (17, 18) to bio-androids (Cell). Infinite energy reactors grant unlimited stamina. Gamma 1 and 2 represent the latest generation of Red Ribbon technology.

View Details

Kai (Shinjin)

Divine beings born from the Kaiju fruit on the World Core planet. Supreme Kais watch over creation while King Kais govern quadrants of the universe. Represented by Shin, King Kai, Elder Kai, and Zamasu. Golden fruit produces Supreme Kai candidates; regular fruit produces standard Kais. Life-linked to Gods of Destruction.

View Details

Angel Race

The highest-ranking divine beings serving as attendants to Gods of Destruction. Possess perfect Ultra Instinct and power far exceeding their destroyer masters. Represented by Whis, Vados, Maracrita, and the Grand Minister. Bound by angelic law to remain neutral and never engage in combat directly.

View Details

God of Destruction

Deities appointed to destroy planets, civilizations, and threats to maintain universal balance. Each universe has one GoD served by an angel attendant. Represented by Beerus (Universe 7), Champa (Universe 6), Belmod (Universe 11). Their power is bound to the life of their universe's Supreme Kai.

View Details

Z-Fighters

Earth's premier defense team originally formed to combat the Saiyan threat. Core members include Goku, Vegeta, Piccolo, Gohan, Krillin, Tien, and Android 18. This loose alliance of former enemies and rivals has defended Earth against Frieza, Cell, Buu, and universe-ending threats for decades.

View Details

Red Ribbon Army

A global paramilitary organization led by Commander Red seeking the Dragon Balls for world domination. Dr. Gero's android program produced the most dangerous creations from this army. Revived in Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero under Magenta's leadership, creating Gamma 1, Gamma 2, and Cell Max.

View Details

Frieza Force

An intergalactic military empire founded by King Cold and expanded by Frieza. Controls over 448 planets through conquest and planet-brokering. The Ginyu Force, Zarbon, Dodoria, and the Saiyan Army were its most notorious units. Resurrected after the Tournament of Power with Frieza returning to command.

View Details

Ginyu Force

Frieza's elite five-man squadron of flamboyant super-warriors. Captain Ginyu's Body Change, Jeice's Crusher Ball, Burter's speed, Recoome's brute force, and Guldo's time-freezing telekinesis made them the most feared strike team in the Frieza Force until Goku dismantled them on Namek.

View Details

Pride Troopers

Universe 11's heroic justice organization protecting their universe's planets. Jiren, Toppo (a God of Destruction candidate), and Dyspo are their strongest members. Operate under a strict code of justice and honor, contrasting with Universe 7's informal Z-Fighter approach to planetary defense.

View Details

Galactic Patrol

An interstellar law enforcement agency policing the Milky Way galaxy. Jaco is their Earth liaison, while Merus was secretly an angel-in-training. Imprisoned Moro for millions of years before his escape. Recruited Goku and Vegeta as temporary deputized officers to combat the Moro threat.

View Details

Heeter Force

A family-based criminal syndicate operating as information brokers and power dealers throughout Universe 7. Gas, the strongest Heeter, made a wish to become the universe's top warrior using the Cerealian Dragon Balls. Led by Elec, who manipulated events to eliminate Frieza's empire and seize control.

View Details

Time Patrol

An organization from the future tasked with preserving the integrity of the timeline. Xeno Trunks and Chronoa (Supreme Kai of Time) lead agents who correct temporal anomalies and prevent history-altering threats. Featured prominently in Dragon Ball Xenoverse and Super Dragon Ball Heroes.
