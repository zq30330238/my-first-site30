Super Saiyan Transformations

⭐

Super Saiyan (SSJ)

50x base power multiplier. Golden hair, green eyes. First achieved by Goku on Namek after Krillin's death. The iconic transformation that defined a generation.

💫

Super Saiyan Blue (SSB / SSGSS)

Super Saiyan God + Super Saiyan combined. Cyan-blue hair. The primary form used throughout Dragon Ball Super. Requires mastery of godly ki control.

🌟

Ultra Instinct (MUI)

The technique of the angels. Silver-white hair, autonomous body movement. Goku's ultimate form — each part of the body reacts independently to threats without thought.
