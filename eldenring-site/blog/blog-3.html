Malenia Boss Guide: How to Defeat the Blade of Miquella

March 25, 2025 · 7 min read

Understanding Malenia's Moveset

Malenia is one of the hardest bosses in Elden Ring. She uses a katana with a long reach and fast combos. Her signature move is Waterfowl Dance, a flurry of slashes that can kill you in seconds. When she starts floating in the air, roll away immediately. The first two flurries cover a wide area. Roll directly into her for the third flurry, then roll away for the fourth. Another dangerous move is the Scarlet Aeonia, where she turns into a giant rot flower. Run away as soon as you see the animation, then wait for the rot cloud to dissipate before re-engaging. Her thrust attacks are punishable with a quick roll and counterattack. Stay close to her but maintain enough distance to react to her quick startups.

Recommended Builds and Equipment

Your best bet against Malenia is a bleed build. Use weapons like Rivers of Blood or Morgott's Cursed Sword. Both apply bleed quickly and can stagger her. Alternatively, use a greatshield with high guard boost, like the Fingerprint Stone Shield, paired with a thrusting weapon. This lets you block most of her attacks and counter. For armor, equip the heaviest set you can wear while staying at medium equip load. Boost your fire resistance with the Flamedrake Talisman +2 to reduce damage from her fire-based attacks. Use the Crimson Amber Medallion +2 for extra HP. The Lord of Blood's Exultation talisman gives a 20% damage boost when bleed procs. Summon Spirit Ashes like Black Knife Tiche or Mimic Tear to distract her. Upgrade your weapons to +23 or higher before the fight.

Pro tip: Use a frost build with the Chill Whirlwind Ash of War on a straight sword. Frost buildup slows her movement and lowers her defense. Once frost procs, switch to a fire weapon to reset the frost and reapply it for bonus damage.

Phase Two: Goddess of Rot

At 50% HP, Malenia triggers her second phase. She explodes into Scarlet Aeonia, dealing massive rot damage. Run to the edge of the arena to avoid the initial blast. After the explosion, she spawns a phantom version of herself that copies her attacks. Focus on the real Malenia while dodging the phantom. Her moves are faster and more aggressive. She adds a new combo: a series of quick slashes followed by a sweep. Roll through the sweep to get behind her for a backstab. Her grab attack becomes instant; never stay in front of her for long. Use ranged attacks when she summons rot clones. Keep healing items like Boluses and a shield with rot protection. If you get caught in the rot cloud, use the Flame, Cleanse Me incantation or boluses immediately. The fight is a marathon. Conserve stamina and don't get greedy. One or two hits at a time is safe. When she winds up for the Waterfowl Dance, sprint away to gain distance. With practice and the right build, you can stagger her repeatedly and break her stance.
