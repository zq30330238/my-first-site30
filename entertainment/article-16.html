Celebrity Relationships: Who's Dating Who

May 21, 2026 6 min read

Celebrity Relationships: Who's Dating Who – The Hottest Couples and Rumors of the Moment

Hollywood’s love carousel never stops spinning, and right now it’s whirling faster than a TMZ headline. From surprise engagements to suspiciously cozy coffee dates, the rumor mill is churning out piping-hot gossip. Whether you live for PDA on yachts or subtle Instagram hints, we’ve got the inside scoop on which A-listers are locking lips, which power couples are calling it quits, and which fledgling flings are about to explode. Grab your magnifying glass (and maybe a glass of rosé) – it’s time to decode the romance radar.

The Rebound Revolution: Splits, Surprises, and Swift Moves

First up, the breakup dominoes are tumbling faster than anyone expected. After months of speculation, pop princess Ariana Grande and real estate mogul Dalton Gomez officially filed for divorce in September – but the rebound express had already left the station. Grande was spotted multiple times with Broadway star Ethan Slater, her Wicked co-star, leading to whispers of a set-side romance. Sources say the two are “inseparable” between rehearsals, and Slater’s estranged wife has reportedly been blindsided. Meanwhile, Taylor Swift continues to dominate both charts and headlines with her own rebound narrative: after her split from Joe Alwyn earlier this year, she’s been linked to Matty Healy (The 1975), and more recently, NFL superstar Travis Kelce. Yes, you read that right – Swift was seen cheering in a luxury box at Arrowhead Stadium, and Kelce’s shirtless podcast appearance may or may not have been a not-so-subtle flex. The internet is calling it the crossover event of the decade. And it doesn’t stop there – Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth are back in the conversation after a very public, very close encounter at a charity gala. Are they rekindling? A source close to Cyrus says, “They’re just friendly, but they both know nobody else gets them like that.” Ambiguous? Yes. Delicious? Absolutely.

“Hollywood relationships are like seasons in a single day: one minute it's summer love, the next it's a winter breakup. The only guarantee is that someone, somewhere, is taking mirror selfies with a new arm candy.”
— Entertainment Buzz Confidential, 2024

New Power Couples Rise: Zendaya & Tom Holland, Kylie & Timothée

While some couples crash and burn, others are cementing their status as the reigning monarchs of young Hollywood. Zendaya and Tom Holland have been together since the Spider-Man: Homecoming days, but their relationship has only grown more solid – and more private. Despite the occasional red-carpet appearance (remember that matching Bottega moment?), they’ve mastered the art of staying out of the gossip glare. Insiders say they’re “ring shopping,” but no official confirmation yet. Meanwhile, the internet lost its collective mind over Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet. Polarizing? Sure. Unstoppable? Apparently. After months of “just friends” denials, they were photographed holding hands at a Beyoncé concert, and later canoodling at a private film screening. Chalamet’s team has been tight-lipped, but Jenner’s makeup artist accidentally confirmed the romance by liking a fan edit. These two are either a viral marketing stunt or the oddest-couple-turned-heartthrob-dream-team. Either way, they’re everywhere – and we’re not mad about it. Also making waves: Dua Lipa and Callum Turner. The singer and the Masters of the Air actor were seen kissing backstage at Glastonbury, and now they’re practically glued to each other at every fashion week. One source described them as “the most stylish and smitten couple in London right now.”

Red Carpet Romances: Awards Season’s Secret Signals

Speaking of stylish, the fall award circuit has been a breeding ground for unexpected pairings. At the Emmys, Selena Gomez was seen laughing a little too long with Andrew Garfield – who, by the way, split from his long-time girlfriend earlier this year. Fans on X (formerly Twitter) went into overdrive analyzing body language, but sources claim they’re “just friends.” However, Garfield has been notoriously private about his love life, so a whisper of a romance is enough to send tabloids into a frenzy. Another red-carpet moment that had tongues wagging: Megan Thee Stallion and Peso Pluma sitting front row at Paris Fashion Week, locked in what can only be described as “a whispered conversation with intense eye contact.” Neither has commented, but their teams haven’t denied it either. And let’s not forget Bella Hadid and Adan Banuelos – the model and the horse trainer? Yes, you heard that right. Bella has been spending more time on her ranch than on the runway, and sources say Banuelos is “the man who finally makes her feel grounded.” Who knew the real love story was hidden in the stables?

Quick Hit: Five Couples You Forgot Were Still Together (Or Not)

Sometimes the most shocking news is that some stars have actually managed to keep their relationships stable. Here’s a rapid-fire list of couples that might have slipped under your radar:

  • Paul Rudd & Julie Yaeger – Over 20 years married, and he’s still the most beloved man alive. Go figure.
  • Mila Kunis & Ashton Kutcher – Still going strong, even after the Foursquare drama. They’re reportedly trying for baby #3.
  • JLo & Ben Affleck – Yes, still married, but rumors of tension are bubbling. She’s on tour, he’s directing – distance makes the heart wander?
  • Kourtney Kardashian & Travis Barker – They’re more in love than ever, but Barker’s recent medical scare has put a spotlight on their devotion.
  • Pedro Pascal & ? – Nobody knows who he’s dating, but that hasn’t stopped the internet from shipping him with every co-star. The mystery deepens.

Industry Insider: What the Agents Aren’t Saying

Behind the scenes, talent managers and PR teams are working overtime to control narratives. One publicist, speaking on condition of anonymity, revealed that PR relationships are becoming more common – especially for stars promoting high-profile projects. “We’ll set up a few paparazzi ‘sightings’ to bump engagement for a movie drop,” they said. “But the real couples? They’re usually the ones who hate being photographed together. When a couple is too perfect on Instagram, it’s probably a brand deal.” That’s not to say all Hollywood love is manufactured – sometimes the sparks are real, like with Jennifer Lawrence and Cooke Maroney, who recently welcomed their second child and are reportedly “boringly happy.” And then there are the mysterious disappearances: Bradley Cooper and Gigi Hadid have been spotted at a private farm in upstate New York, fueling rumors they’ve taken their relationship off-grid. Are they serious? Only time (and a blurry long-lens photo) will tell.

The Final Forecast: Who Will Be the Couple of 2024?

So, who’s going to dominate the tabloids through the end of the year? Our crystal ball says Swift/Kelce is the surefire headline generator – it has music, sports, and a dash of celebrity drama. But keep an eye on Chalamet and Jenner; their age gap and different worlds make for irresistible content. And if you love a good comeback, Cyrus and Hemsworth might just pull a Bennifer 2.0. Bottom line: single or coupled up, these stars are giving us enough material to fuel brunch gossip for weeks. Stay tuned, stay thirsty – Entertainment Buzz will be here to serve you every steamy update. In the meantime, don't believe everything you read on blind item sites… unless it’s from us.
