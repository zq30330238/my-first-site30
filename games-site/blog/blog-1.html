Raiden Shogun Build Guide: Best Artifacts and Weapons

April 7, 2025 · 6 min read

Overview

Raiden Shogun, the Electro Archon of Inazuma, is one of the most versatile characters in Genshin Impact. She excels as both a main DPS and a support, providing energy regeneration for the entire team while dealing massive burst damage. Her Elemental Skill, Transcendence: Baleful Oath, grants consistent off-field Electro damage and increases the burst damage of nearby party members. Her Elemental Burst, Secret Art: Musou Shinsetsu, delivers a powerful single-hit slash followed by a short window of enhanced attacks that restore energy for the party. Building her correctly requires focusing on Energy Recharge and Crit stats, as her passive converts recharge into Electro damage bonus.

Always aim for at least 250% Energy Recharge on Raiden Shogun. This maximizes her Electro damage bonus from her passive talent and ensures her burst is ready off cooldown.

Best Artifacts for Raiden Shogun

The Emblem of Severed Fate set is her undisputed best-in-slot artifact set. The 2-piece bonus gives 20% Energy Recharge, and the 4-piece increases Elemental Burst damage by 25% of Energy Recharge, up to a maximum of 75%. Since Raiden builds high recharge, she easily caps this bonus. For the main stats, use an Energy Recharge sands, Electro Damage goblet, and Crit Rate or Crit Damage circlet depending on your weapon. Aim for at least 60% Crit Rate and 140% Crit Damage after accounting for substats. Good substats are Energy Recharge, Crit Rate, Crit Damage, and ATK%. If you lack a good Emblem set, a 2-piece Noblesse Oblige with 2-piece Thundering Fury works as a temporary option, but Emblem is significantly stronger.

Best Weapons for Raiden Shogun

Engulfing Lightning is her signature polearm and the top choice. It provides high base ATK, Energy Recharge as a substat, and an effect that converts recharge into ATK while refunding energy after using her burst. The Catch, a free fishing reward from Inazuma, is the best four-star option. It grants Energy Recharge and increases burst damage and crit rate on burst hits. Skyward Spine is another solid five-star pick that offers recharge and normal attack speed. For pure damage, Staff of Homa works well if you can maintain enough recharge from artifacts. Avoid weapons with low recharge unless your artifact substats are exceptional.

Team Compositions

Raiden Shogun fits into many team archetypes. The most popular is the Rational team: Raiden, Xiangling, Xingqiu, and Bennett. Raiden acts as the driver and battery, enabling Xiangling and Xingqiu to spam their bursts. In hypercarry teams, pair her with Kujou Sara, Kazuha, and Bennett for massive Electro damage. She also works in Eula teams, where her burst damage and energy regeneration help Eula maintain uptime on her physical burst. For a budget option, use Raiden, Xiangling, Fischl, and Barbara for overload reactions and healing. Always ensure your supports have enough energy to feed Raiden's burst rotation.

Playstyle Tips

Start your rotation by using Raiden's Elemental Skill, then swap to supports to activate their bursts. After applying all buffs, switch back to Raiden and unleash her burst. During her burst's enhanced stance, perform 3 normal attacks followed by a charged attack to maximize damage and energy generation. Use her skill reactively to trigger Electro reactions for additional damage. Remember that her skill's coordinated attacks have a 0.9-second cooldown, so fast attack sequences are wasted. Manage your energy carefully to keep her burst available for every rotation. With proper investment, Raiden Shogun will dominate both single-target and AoE encounters.
