Mirage: The Ultimate Bamboozle Guide

April 4, 2025 · 5 min read

Understanding Mirage's Toolkit

Mirage's strength comes from deception. His passive, Encore!, drops a decoy that mimics his death animation when he's knocked down. Use this to crawl away to safety while enemies waste ammo on the hologram. The tactical ability, Psyche Out, sends a controllable decoy that runs in a straight line. You can fake a sprint by sending a decoy in one direction while you duck for cover. His ultimate, Life of the Party, creates a ring of five decoys that mimic your every move for a few seconds. This is your best tool for confusing entire squads during a push or escape.

Advanced Bamboozle Strategies

To maximize Mirage's kit, use decoys to scout ahead. Send a decoy into a building or around a corner to check for enemies. If it gets shot, you know someone's there. Use his ultimate before reviving a teammate: the decoys make it nearly impossible for enemies to spot the real revive. For aggressive plays, pop the ultimate as you round a corner and slide into a group. The enemy team will split their attention, giving you easy shots. Always remember that enemies can see a faint outline of the real Mirage, so keep moving erratically.

Pro Tip: When using his ultimate, slide or jump immediately after activating. The decoys will follow your movement, making it even harder for enemies to track the real Mirage.

Synergies and Counters

Mirage works well with aggressive legends like Octane or Bloodhound. Octane's stim allows you to reposition faster after a bamboozle, while Bloodhound's scan can reveal enemies you've fooled. Pairing with Lifeline is also strong: Mirage revives while invisible, and Lifeline's drone can heal in the open. Avoid using decoys near Caustic gas or Wattson fences, as they can reveal your location. Crypto's drone can also spot your decoys and identify the real you. In those matchups, use decoys sparingly and rely on your ultimate for group confusion.

Practice in the Firing Range to get comfortable with decoy control. Bind a key to quickly send decoys in different directions. Remember: a decoy that jumps over a rock or climbs a wall looks more realistic than one that runs straight. Use terrain to sell the illusion. With enough practice, you'll bamboozle your way to Apex Predator.
