Omnioculus Nightstalker Build for Grandmaster Nightfalls

March 26, 2025 · 6 min read

Why Omnioculus?

Omnioculus turns the Nightstalker subclass into a survival engine in high-difficulty PvE content. The exotic chest piece grants two smoke bomb charges and provides 50% damage resistance to nearby allies when you invis them. In Grandmaster Nightfalls, where a single sniper shot can end a run, this resist buff keeps your team alive during revives or tight repositioning. The double melee charge also means you can chain invisibility almost indefinitely with proper ability management.

Key Mods and Stats

Prioritize Mobility (100) to reduce dodge cooldown, since Marksman's Dodge pairs with the Sixth Coyote or Omnioculus? Actually, use Gambler's Dodge to refund your melee. Then stack Resilience (100) for the max damage reduction bonus from the Resilience stat. Discipline to 100 completes the build. For mods, equip Bomber on your class item to generate grenade energy on dodge. Use double Utility Kickstart on your cloak for faster dodge cooldowns. For combat style mods, Charged Up, Stacks on Stacks, and Supercharged let you hold 4 stacks of Charged with Light. Use that with Heavy Handed to generate ammo and melee energy on close range kills.

Pro tip: Weave grenade throws between smoke bombs to abuse the Echo of Provision fragment, which gives dodge energy on grenade damage. This loop keeps your invis uptime nearly constant.

Team Synergy

Omnioculus excels when your team stays close. Coordinate using an Arc subclass teammate with Heart of Inmost Light for ability spam, or a Well-lock for area denial. Your role is to mark dangerous targets with Tethers - but the invis is the main draw. Always save one smoke charge for emergency revives. When the orbs from your team's supers start flowing, you can switch to the Reaper mod to generate more orbs from kills. The key is to never stay visible longer than necessary. Pop invis before peeking lanes, and use your dodge reactively to break aim assist from enemies.
