About Hunter x Hunter Wiki

Hunter x Hunter Wiki is a comprehensive fan resource dedicated to the acclaimed manga and anime series Hunter x Hunter, created by Yoshihiro Togashi. Our mission is to provide fans with detailed, accurate, and up-to-date information about the world of Hunters, Nen, and the incredible characters that make this series one of the greatest shonen stories ever told.

From the adventurous spirit of Gon Freecss to the complex morality of the Phantom Troupe, from the intricate Nen system to the breathtaking Chimera Ant arc — we cover every aspect of the Hunter x Hunter universe. Our content is created by passionate fans for passionate fans.

Hunter x Hunter first debuted in Weekly Shonen Jump in 1998 and has since become one of the best-selling manga series of all time. Despite periodic hiatuses, the series has maintained an incredibly dedicated fanbase worldwide, praised for its complex characters, strategic combat system, and mature storytelling that evolves significantly over its run.

Our Mission

We believe Hunter x Hunter deserves a resource that matches its depth and complexity. Our goal is to create the most comprehensive English-language guide to the series, with detailed character profiles, Nen ability explanations, arc summaries, and analysis of the series' deeper themes.
