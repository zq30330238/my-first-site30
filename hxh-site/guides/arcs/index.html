Story Arcs

Relive the journey of Gon and his friends through every major story arc.

Hunter Exam Arc

Hunter Exam Arc

The story begins as Gon Freecss leaves his home on Whale Island to become a Hunter. Along the way, he meets Killua Zoldyck, Kurapika, and Leorio Paradinight. Together, they face the grueling Hunter Exam — a series of deadly tests designed to weed out all but the most determined candidates.

Highlights: This arc establishes the main characters and the world of Hunters. Key events include the first meeting of the main four, the trick tower challenge, and Hisoka's introduction.

Zoldyck Family Arc

Zoldyck Family Arc

After the Hunter Exam, Gon and his friends travel to the Zoldyck family estate to retrieve Killua, who was taken back by his family. They must overcome the family's defenses and prove their worth to free Killua from his family's control.

Highlights: This short arc explores Killua's background and the deadly Zoldyck family. It introduces key family members including Silva, Zeno, and Illumi Zoldyck.

Heavens Arena Arc

Heavens Arena Arc

Gon and Killua travel to Heavens Arena, a massive skyscraper where fighters compete in televised battles. Here, they learn about Nen from the martial arts master Wing. This arc serves as the training ground where the boys begin to understand aura and combat Nen.

Highlights: This arc is where the Nen system is fully introduced and explained. It features Gon and Killua's first serious Nen training and their battles against opponents who use Nen.

Yorknew City Arc

Yorknew City Arc

Kurapika's quest for revenge against the Phantom Troupe comes to a head in the bustling metropolis of Yorknew City. The arc interweaves multiple storylines as the Troupe, the Mafia, and our heroes clash over valuable auction items.

Highlights: Considered one of the best arcs in anime, Yorknew City showcases Kurapika's Chain Jail, Chrollo's abilities, and the full might of the Phantom Troupe. It is darker and more mature than previous arcs.

Greed Island Arc

Greed Island Arc

Gon and Killua enter Greed Island, a game created by Ging Freecss that exists in the real world. They must clear the game to learn more about Ging. Under the tutelage of Biscuit Krueger, they hone their Nen abilities while navigating the game's challenges.

Highlights: This arc focuses on training and character growth. Gon and Killua master advanced Nen techniques while building a unique card collection. It bridges the lighter early arcs with the darkness to come.

Chimera Ant Arc

Chimera Ant Arc

The longest and most complex arc of Hunter x Hunter. Chimera Ants — creatures that eat other beings and evolve by inheriting their traits — threaten humanity. The Hunter Association launches a full-scale invasion of the Ant kingdom, leading to an epic confrontation between Netero and Meruem.

Highlights: This arc explores themes of humanity, evolution, and sacrifice. It features the darkest moments in the series and is widely regarded as a masterpiece of storytelling. The arc includes the Royal Guards, Gon's transformation, and the tragic end of Meruem.
