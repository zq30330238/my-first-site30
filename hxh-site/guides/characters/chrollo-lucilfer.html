Chrollo Lucilfer

The enigmatic leader of the Phantom Troupe, a thief with the ability to steal others' Nen abilities.

← All Characters

Overview

Chrollo Lucilfer is the founder and leader of the Phantom Troupe, a group of S-class criminals. Born in Meteor City, he formed the Troupe to steal and avenge the death of their friend. He is methodical, intelligent, and views the Troupe as his family. His ultimate goal is to steal everything he finds beautiful or valuable.

Appearance

Chrollo has dark hair parted in the middle and dark eyes. He has a distinctive cross-shaped tattoo on his forehead that extends to his temples. He typically wears a long black coat with white fur trim and carries a book labeled 'Bandit's Secret.' His appearance is calm and composed, rarely showing emotion.

Personality

Chrollo is calm, collected, and highly intelligent. He speaks softly and rarely raises his voice, even in battle. He values the Phantom Troupe above all and treats each member as family. Despite being a criminal, he has a code of honor and is surprisingly philosophical, often contemplating the nature of value and beauty.

Abilities

Chrollo is a Specialist who uses Bandit's Secret (Skill Hunter), a book that allows him to steal Nen abilities from others. He can use any stolen ability freely but can only use one at a time. Over the years, he has amassed a vast collection of abilities. He is also highly skilled in hand-to-hand combat and strategy.

Trivia

Chrollo's design was inspired by the character Feitan from Togashi's earlier work. His forehead cross is a tattoo, not a scar. The Bandit's Secret book contains the names and descriptions of all the abilities he has stolen throughout his career.
