Gon Freecss

A young boy who discovers his father is a legendary Hunter. He sets out to take the Hunter Exam and find him.

← All Characters

Overview

Gon Freecss is the main protagonist of Hunter x Hunter. He is a young boy who was raised on Whale Island by his Aunt Mito. After learning that his father Ging is a legendary Hunter still alive, Gon decides to take the Hunter Exam to find him. Throughout his journey, he makes lifelong friends, faces deadly enemies, and discovers the wonders of Nen.

Appearance

Gon has spiky black hair and large, expressive dark brown eyes. He is short for his age but athletic. His typical outfit consists of a green and yellow jacket, green shorts, and boots. As the series progresses, his appearance matures, especially during the Chimera Ant arc when he undergoes a terrifying transformation driven by rage.

Personality

Gon is optimistic, straightforward, and incredibly friendly. He makes friends easily due to his open-hearted nature and unwavering loyalty. However, he can also be stubborn and reckless, especially when his friends are threatened. His simplicity hides a sharp intuition that often surprises his opponents. His greatest flaw is his capacity for immense rage when pushed too far.

Abilities

Gon is a natural Enhancer, making his Nen type focused on increasing physical strength. His signature abilities include Jajanken (Rock-Paper-Scissors), which channels his enhancement into powerful punches. He also develops exceptional senses, especially smell, and incredible physical endurance. During the Chimera Ant arc, he makes a Nen vow that grants him immense power at a terrible cost.

Trivia

Gon shares his Japanese voice actor with Naruto Uzumaki in some media. His name is a pun on the word 'gon' meaning 'to go'. He is ranked as one of the most popular shonen protagonists in numerous anime polls worldwide.
