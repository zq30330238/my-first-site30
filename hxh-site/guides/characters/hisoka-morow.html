Hisoka Morow

A mysterious and bloodthirsty magician who lives only for battle with strong opponents.

← All Characters

Overview

Hisoka Morow is one of the most enigmatic characters in Hunter x Hunter. He is a rogue Hunter and former member of the Phantom Troupe who lives solely for the thrill of combat. Hisoka is obsessively interested in fighting strong opponents, and he specifically targets Gon and Killua as future challengers worthy of his attention.

Appearance

Hisoka has pale skin, red hair styled upward, and golden eyes with slitted pupils. He wears clown-like makeup, including a star on his cheek and tears beneath his eyes. His attire consists of a sleeveless shirt, pants, and a distinctive cape. His appearance is designed to be unsettling and reflects his unpredictable nature.

Personality

Hisoka is sadistic, cunning, and completely unpredictable. He follows only his own desires, which center around finding and battling powerful opponents. Despite his menacing nature, he has a code of honor regarding fights and dislikes fighting those he considers inferior. He is patient when it comes to grooming future opponents like Gon and Killua.

Abilities

Hisoka is a Transmuter who uses Bungee Gum, a Nen ability that gives his aura both rubber-like elasticity and gum-like stickiness. His Texture Surprise allows him to alter the appearance of any surface. His fighting style is creative and unpredictable, combining acrobatics with his versatile Nen abilities.

Trivia

Hisoka consistently ranks among the top 10 most popular anime villains worldwide. His Japanese voice actor also voices Shigure in Fruits Basket. His character design was inspired by the concept of a 'clown magician' and was refined multiple times by author Yoshihiro Togashi.
