Kurapika

The last survivor of the Kurta Clan, seeking vengeance against the Phantom Troupe who slaughtered his people.

← All Characters

Overview

Kurapika is one of the main protagonists and the last surviving member of the Kurta Clan. The Phantom Troupe massacred his clan to steal their Scarlet Eyes, which turn red when the Kurta experience strong emotions. Kurapika becomes a Hunter to gain the resources and power needed to track down and defeat the Troupe.

Appearance

Kurapika has blonde hair and light blue eyes that turn scarlet red when he is emotionally aroused. He typically dresses in traditional Japanese clothing or formal suits. His appearance is androgynous, often being mistaken for a woman by other characters. After becoming a Hunter, his outfit becomes more practical for combat.

Personality

Kurapika is intelligent, calm, and highly analytical. He is driven by a deep sense of purpose and justice, but his obsession with revenge often clouds his judgment. Despite his cold exterior, he deeply cares for his friends, especially those he met during the Hunter Exam. His greatest struggle is balancing his desire for revenge with his humanity.

Abilities

Kurapika is a Conjurer who created chains specifically designed to defeat the Phantom Troupe. His Chain Jail binds Troupe members and forces them into Zetsu state. He also has Dowsing Chain for tracking and healing, and Holy Chain for exorcism. Using Emperor Time, he can utilize all Nen types at 100% efficiency when his eyes turn scarlet.

Trivia

Kurapika's scarlet eyes are distinct from the Sharingan in Naruto, though both involve red eyes. His chain abilities are heavily inspired by Buddhist prayer beads. He is the most strategic fighter in the main group, often winning through planning rather than brute force.
