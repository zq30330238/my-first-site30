Leorio Paradinight

A aspiring Hunter who wants to become a doctor to help the poor and make the world a better place.

← All Characters

Overview

Leorio Paradinight is one of the main protagonists. Unlike his friends who seek adventure or revenge, Leorio wants to become a Hunter primarily for the financial benefits that will allow him to attend medical school. His ultimate goal is to become a doctor and provide free healthcare to those who cannot afford it.

Appearance

Leorio is tall with short black hair and brown eyes. He typically wears a tan trench coat, a pink or purple suit, and sunglasses. He looks much older than his actual age, often being mistaken for an adult. His mature appearance contrasts with his sometimes childish behavior.

Personality

Leorio is hot-headed, greedy, and easily frustrated, but he has a good heart. He often argues with Kurapika and others but genuinely cares for his friends. His motivation to become a doctor reveals his compassionate nature. He matures significantly over the series, becoming more composed and focused on his goals.

Abilities

Leorio is an Emitter and developed a unique ability called Warping Punch, which allows him to deliver powerful punches at a distance by warping space. Despite limited combat training compared to his friends, he is physically capable and has strong Nen potential. His medical knowledge also proves useful in various situations.

Trivia

In the 1999 anime adaptation, Leorio's backstory involving his sick friend was expanded. He often serves as comic relief but has serious moments that remind viewers of his dedication. His character represents the theme that not all Hunters need to be fighters.
