Machi Komachine

A skilled Phantom Troupe member whose Nen threads can stitch wounds and trap enemies.

← All Characters

Overview

Machi Komachine is a member of the Phantom Troupe, known for her demeanor and practical skills. She is one of the original members from Meteor City and specializes in using Nen threads for both healing and combat. She is often paired with Nobunaga on missions.

Appearance

Machi has short purple hair and blue eyes. She has a slim, athletic build and typically wears casual clothing appropriate for infiltration missions. Her most distinctive feature is the Troupe's spider tattoo on her body. She rarely wears flashy outfits, preferring practicality.

Personality

Machi is calm, practical, and no-nonsense. She rarely shows emotion and speaks in a direct manner. Despite being a criminal, she has a sense of loyalty to the Troupe and follows Chrollo's leadership without question. She is level-headed even in crisis situations.

Abilities

Machi is a Transmuter who creates Nen threads that are incredibly strong and can be used for stitching wounds, restraining opponents, or creating traps. Her threads can also be used for telepathic communication over distances. Her medical stitching ability makes her one of the Troupe's most valuable members.

Trivia

Machi was the one who stitched Hisoka's severed arm back on during the Yorknew City arc. Her threads are strong enough to restrain even powerful Nen users. She has a complicated history with Hisoka, bordering on mutual respect mixed with distrust.
