Menthuthuyoupi

The rage-driven Royal Guard whose emotions fuel his ever-growing power and transformation.

← All Characters

Overview

Menthuthuyoupi is one of the three Royal Guards of Meruem. Born as a Chimera Ant with immense physical power, Youpi is driven primarily by his emotions. As he learns to control his rage, he becomes one of the most formidable fighters in the series, capable of transforming his body in combat.

Appearance

Youpi has a large muscular humanoid body with dark red skin and a horn protruding from his forehead. He has multiple eyes and no visible mouth. His form is constantly shifting and evolving as he grows in combat. After transforming, he becomes more humanoid and gains additional features.

Personality

Youpi is initially driven purely by rage and instinct, but through his battle with the Hunter team, he develops intelligence and strategic thinking. He learns to control his emotions and even shows compassion. His growth from a mindless brute to a strategic warrior is remarkable.

Abilities

Youpi is a Conjurer/Transmuter hybrid. His ability Rage Blast releases explosive energy from his body. He can also transform his body freely, creating weapons and shields. His Pouf-given ability allows him to split his body into independent copies. His power grows exponentially with his emotional intensity.

Trivia

Youpi's name is derived from 'Youpi' which relates to the sound of an explosion in Japanese. Among the Royal Guards, he has the most raw physical strength. His character arc shows that intelligence can be developed, not just inherited.
