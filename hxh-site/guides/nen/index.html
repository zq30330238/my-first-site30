The Nen System

Nen is the ability to control one's own life energy (aura). There are six distinct Nen types, each with unique properties.

Enhancement

Enhancer

Enhancers are Nen users who specialize in strengthening their own physical abilities. They focus their aura to enhance their natural attributes like strength, speed, and durability. Enhancement is the most straightforward and balanced Nen type.

Representative: Gon Freecss

Tendency: Enhancers are typically simple-minded, honest, and determined. They prefer direct confrontation over complex strategies. Notable Enhancers include Gon Freecss and Isaac Netero.

Transmutation

Transmuter

Transmuters change the properties of their aura to mimic physical substances. By altering their aura's characteristics, they can create electricity, rubber, fire, or other properties. This type requires immense creativity and visualization.

Representative: Killua Zoldyck

Tendency: Transmuters are often whimsical, eccentric, or deceptive. They enjoy surprising others with their creative abilities. Notable Transmuters include Killua Zoldyck and Hisoka Morow.

Emission

Emitter

Emitters can separate their aura from their body and maintain its power over long distances. This type is ideal for ranged attacks, projectiles, and aura-based constructs that operate independently.

Representative: Leorio Paradinight

Tendency: Emitters are typically hot-headed, impatient, and straightforward. They prefer quick resolutions and direct approaches. Notable Emitters include Leorio Paradinight and Franklin Bordeau.

Conjuration

Conjurer

Conjurers can create physical objects from their aura. These conjured items can have special properties and abilities. Conjuration requires precise visualization and detailed understanding of the object being created.

Representative: Kurapika

Tendency: Conjurers are often serious, tense, and highly analytical. They prefer complex strategies and careful planning. Notable Conjurers include Kurapika and Kite.

Manipulation

Manipulator

Manipulators can control living beings or objects using their Nen. They impose their will on others, controlling movements, thoughts, or natural phenomena. This type is powerful but requires direct contact or consistent aura application.

Representative: Illumi Zoldyck

Tendency: Manipulators are often logical and possessive. They prefer control and order in their lives. Notable Manipulators include Illumi Zoldyck and Shaiapouf.

Specialization

Specialist

Specialists are Nen users whose abilities don't fit into the other five categories. Their powers are unique and often involve manipulating time, space, or other abstract concepts. Specialization is the rarest Nen type.

Representative: Meruem

Tendency: Specialists are often charismatic, independent, and self-centered. They follow their own rules and paths. Notable Specialists include Chrollo Lucilfer and Neferpitou.
