Story Arcs

Follow the complete Jujutsu Kaisen storyline through its major arcs, from the prequel movie to the Culling Games.

Movie / Volumes 0

Jujutsu Kaisen 0

The prequel story follows Yuta Okkotsu, a timid boy haunted by his childhood friend Rika's vengeful spirit. Satoru Gojo recruits him to Tokyo Jujutsu High, where Yuta learns to control his immense cursed energy. The arc culminates in a confrontation with Suguru Geto's Night Parade of a Hundred Demons.

Key Events

Yuta's introduction, Rika's curse revealed, training at Jujutsu High, Geto's invasion of Jujutsu High, Yuta vs Geto, Rika's ascension

Episodes 1-8

Cursed Child Arc

Yuji Itadori, a high school student with superhuman strength, encounters Megumi Fushiguro and becomes entangled in the world of jujutsu. After swallowing a cursed finger containing Ryomen Sukuna, Yuji is sentenced to execution but receives a stay of execution to collect all 20 of Sukuna's fingers.

Key Events

Yuji meets Megumi, swallowing Sukuna's finger, execution sentence, joining Jujutsu High, introduction of Gojo, Nobara, and the first missions

Episodes 9-20

Vs. Mahito Arc

Yuji and his friends take on various missions that introduce them to the Disaster Curses. Yuji befriends Junpei Yoshino, a target of Mahito's manipulation. The arc explores the nature of curses and the tragedy of those caught between the human and cursed worlds.

Key Events

Junpei's introduction, Mahito's first appearance, Nanami's mentorship, Junpei's death, Yuji vs Mahito, Gojo's confrontation with the Disaster Curses

Episodes 21-29

Kyoto Goodwill Event Arc

Tokyo and Kyoto Jujutsu Highs face off in the annual Goodwill Event, a competition designed to strengthen both schools. The event is disrupted by the Disaster Curses, leading to intense battles and the revelation of Mechamaru's betrayal.

Key Events

Team competition, Todo's alliance with Yuji, Mechamaru's betrayal revealed, Hanami's attack, Gojo vs Hanami, school collaboration

Episodes 30-53

Shibuya Incident Arc

The Disaster Curses enact a massive plan to seal Gojo in the Prison Realm, triggering a devastating battle in the heart of Shibuya. This arc is the turning point of the series, with massive casualties, betrayals, and the emergence of chaos in the jujutsu world.

Key Events

Gojo sealed in Prison Realm, Nanami's death, Mahito's evolution, Yuji's mental breakdown, Sukuna's rampage, Toji's reanimation, Gojo's release and destruction

Volumes 14-16

Itadori's Execution Arc

In the aftermath of Shibuya, Yuji becomes a wanted fugitive. The jujutsu higher-ups order his execution, forcing him to go on the run. Yuta Okkotsu returns with a mission to kill Yuji if necessary, while Yuji seeks to master his abilities and regain control of his fate.

Key Events

Yuji's fugitive status, Yuta's return, Yuji and Choso alliance, training with Kusakabe, Culling Games preparation

Volumes 17-23

Culling Games Arc

Kenjaku's master plan unfolds as he activates the Culling Games, a massive battle royale across Japan. Players fight for points in cursed technique-filled colonies, with deadly consequences for failure. Multiple factions clash as the true scope of Kenjaku's scheme becomes clear.

Key Events

Culling Games begin, colony battles, Hakari vs Kashimo, Maki's Zenin Clan massacre, Yuji and Higuruma, Kenjaku's reveal, Tsumiki's tragedy
