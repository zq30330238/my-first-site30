Choso

Death Painting Womb

Affiliation: None (Independent)

Rank: Special Grade Cursed Spirit

Overview

Choso is one of the Death Painting Wombs — a hybrid of human and curse created by Kenjaku. He was originally contained as a painting until he manifested as a fully realized being. His bond with his brothers Eso and Kechizu drives his actions, and he later develops a complex relationship with Yuji, believing them to be brothers.

Appearance

Choso appears as a young man with pale skin, dark markings around his eyes, and black hair tied in a topknot with distinctive streaks. He has dark, soulful eyes that reveal his emotional nature. He wears a traditional dark kimono-style outfit with a high collar and a dark cape.

Personality

Choso is emotional, brooding, and intensely loyal to his family. The loss of his brothers Eso and Kechizu fills him with grief and a desire for revenge that eventually transforms into a protective instinct toward Yuji. He is one of the few cursed spirits capable of deep emotional complexity.

Abilities

Choso uses Blood Manipulation techniques inherited from the Kamo Clan. He can control his own blood as weapons, creating Piercing Blood (super-pressurized blood shots), Blood Edge, and Supernova. He also uses Flowing Red Scale to enhance his physical abilities. His blood has unique properties due to his hybrid nature.

Trivia

Choso was created by Kenjaku as part of an experiment to fuse humans and curses. He considers Yuji his brother because they share the same 'parent' in Kenjaku. His emotional breakdown after sensing his brothers' deaths is one of the most poignant moments in the Shibuya arc.
