Hanami

Nature Spirit

Affiliation: Disaster Curses

Rank: Special Grade Cursed Spirit

Overview

Hanami is a Special Grade Cursed Spirit born from humanity's fear of nature's destruction and natural disasters. As a Disaster Curse, they seek to eliminate humanity to protect the natural world. Hanami's calm and philosophical demeanor contrasts sharply with their genocidal mission.

Appearance

Hanami has dark skin with plant-like patterns, large curved horns, and purple eyes with multiple rings. Their body has wooden textures and floral motifs. They wear a dark robe-like garment and carry a simple wooden staff, resembling a forest deity more than a curse.

Personality

Hanami is calm, philosophical, and speaks with a reverence for nature. They view humans as a plague on the earth and believe that eliminating humanity is the only way to save the planet. Unlike other Disaster Curses, Hanami is not cruel for pleasure — their actions are driven by ecological conviction.

Abilities

Hanami controls plant life and natural elements, using roots, vines, and trees as weapons. They can drain cursed energy from the environment and create forests instantly. Their Domain Expansion, though not shown fully, is implied to be devastating. They also use protective techniques like Simple Domain.

Trivia

Hanami is the only Disaster Curse that uses gender-neutral pronouns. They are deeply connected to the earth and draw power from natural environments. Their death at the hands of Gojo during the Shibuya Incident showcases the overwhelming gap between Special Grade sorcerers and cursed spirits.
