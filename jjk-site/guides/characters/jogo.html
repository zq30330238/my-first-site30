Jogo

Volcano Curse

Affiliation: Disaster Curses

Rank: Special Grade Cursed Spirit

Overview

Jogo is a Special Grade Cursed Spirit born from humanity's fear of volcanoes, earthquakes, and natural catastrophes. As the leader figure among the Disaster Curses, he is fiercely loyal to his cause and possesses immense destructive power. His confrontations with Gojo define much of the power scale in Jujutsu Kaisen.

Appearance

Jogo has pale gray skin, a single large eye with a dark pupil, and a mouth full of sharp teeth that forms a permanent mocking grin. His head resembles a volcano with a cracked, lava-like texture. He wears a dark, traditional robe-like garment and has a stout but imposing figure.

Personality

Jogo is arrogant, ambitious, and deeply prideful about being a Special Grade curse. He despises being looked down upon by sorcerers and yearns to prove that curses are the superior beings. Despite his bravado, he genuinely cares for his fellow Disaster Curses and mourns their losses.

Abilities

Jogo controls volcanic eruptions and extreme heat. He can generate massive lava attacks, create volcanic explosions, and raise temperatures to incinerate everything around him. His Domain Expansion: Coffin of the Iron Mountain is a volcanic hellscape that deals continuous fire damage to all inside.

Trivia

Jogo is killed by Sukuna, who compliments him by saying 'you really are the strongest curse I have encountered in this era,' which Jogo takes as a great honor. His head resembles an active volcano. He is voiced by Shigeru Chiba in Japanese.
