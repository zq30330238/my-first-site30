Kento Nanami

Ratio Technique User

Affiliation: Tokyo Jujutsu High

Rank: Grade 1 Sorcerer

Overview

Kento Nanami is a Grade 1 Jujutsu Sorcerer and former office worker who returned to sorcery after becoming disillusioned with corporate life. He serves as a mentor figure to Yuji and embodies the balance between duty and practicality. His pragmatic approach to curse extermination makes him one of the most reliable sorcerers.

Appearance

Nanami is a tall, well-built man with blonde hair and brown eyes. He is always dressed professionally, wearing a tan trench coat over a business suit, giving him the appearance of a salaryman. His glasses and serious expression complete his mature, professional look.

Personality

Nanami is stoic, pragmatic, and deeply serious. He believes work should not be more than 40 hours a week and complains about overtime even during battles. Despite his cynical exterior, he genuinely cares about protecting the next generation, especially Yuji, for whom he develops a deep respect.

Abilities

Nanami's Ratio Technique divides any target into a 7:3 ratio line, and striking that line guarantees critical damage. He is also skilled in using Black Flash and Simple Domain. His cursed technique is precise and efficient, reflecting his methodical personality.

Trivia

Nanami once quit being a sorcerer to work as an office worker but returned after realizing corporate life was equally soul-crushing. He is known for his catchphrase about overtime. His death during the Shibuya Incident arc is considered one of the most heartbreaking moments in the series.
