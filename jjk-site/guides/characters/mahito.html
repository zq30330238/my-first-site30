Mahito

Curse of Humanity

Affiliation: Disaster Curses

Rank: Special Grade Cursed Spirit

Overview

Mahito is a Special Grade Cursed Spirit born from the fear and hatred humans have toward each other. He represents the most primal and disturbing curses — those born from human cruelty. As a Disaster Curse, he leads attacks against jujutsu society and becomes Yuji's personal nemesis through his grotesque actions.

Appearance

Mahito has pale blue skin, patchwork-like stitches across his body, and an inhuman smile. His form is fluid and constantly shifting, reflecting his ability to manipulate souls. He wears a simple white robe that contrasts with his monstrous appearance.

Personality

Mahito is playful, curious, and utterly sadistic. He views humans as experimental subjects and loves exploring the limits of his power on living victims. His childlike curiosity about souls makes him even more terrifying, as he genuinely enjoys causing suffering without any malice — simply because he finds it interesting.

Abilities

Mahito's Idle Transfiguration allows him to reshape human souls, effectively controlling their bodies and abilities. He can create Transfigured Humans as minions, heal himself by reforming his soul, and use Self-Embodiment of Perfection for offensive attacks. His Domain Expansion: Self-Embodiment of Perfection instantly transfigure any soul within its range.

Trivia

Mahito is responsible for Junpei Yoshino's death, which sets Yuji on a darker path. He evolves rapidly through battle, adapting his techniques. His existence proves that curses born from human hatred of other humans are the most dangerous of all.
