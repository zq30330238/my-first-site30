Kokichi Muta / Mechamaru

Mechanical Marionette User

Affiliation: Kyoto Jujutsu High

Rank: Grade 2 Sorcerer

Overview

Kokichi Muta, known as Mechamaru, is a second-year student at Kyoto Jujutsu High. Born with a Heavenly Restriction that left his real body crippled and bedridden, he remotely controls a mechanical puppet to interact with the world. His brilliant engineering mind and strategic brilliance make him a formidable sorcerer despite his physical limitations.

Appearance

Mechamaru's puppet body is a tall, humanoid robot with a samurai-like helmet and mechanical limbs. His real body is emaciated, pale, and connected to life support tubes, hidden away in a secure location. The contrast between his two bodies reflects the tragedy of his condition.

Personality

Mechamaru is intelligent, methodical, and deeply lonely. His isolation drives him to make a desperate deal with Mahito, betraying his allies in hopes of getting a new, healthy body. Despite his betrayal, he genuinely cares about his teammates and regrets his choices in the end.

Abilities

Mechamaru controls hundreds of mechas across Japan, each equipped with various weapons and sensors. His Ultimate Mech: Absolute Zone is a massive combat robot. He also has the Simple Domain ability and uses stored cursed energy from years of accumulation.

Trivia

Mechamaru's Heavenly Restriction grants him immense cursed energy range at the cost of his physical body. His betrayal during the Kyoto Goodwill Event arc is a pivotal plot twist. His final battle against Mahito is one of the most tragic and heroic moments in the series.
