Megumi Fushiguro

Ten Shadows User

Affiliation: Tokyo Jujutsu High

Rank: Grade 2 Sorcerer

Overview

Megumi Fushiguro is a first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High and a descendant of the Zenin Clan. He is a user of the rare and powerful Ten Shadows Technique, inherited through his family line. Despite his reserved nature, Megumi is a brilliant strategist and possesses immense potential that even Sukuna acknowledges.

Appearance

Megumi has dark, spiky black hair styled upward and sharp blue-green eyes. He is tall and lean with an athletic build. He wears the standard Tokyo Jujutsu High uniform and is rarely seen without a focused, contemplative expression.

Personality

Megumi is calm, introverted, and highly analytical. He often doubts his own worth but is fiercely protective of his allies. He believes his role is to support others rather than shine himself, though this changes as he develops confidence in his abilities.

Abilities

The Ten Shadows Technique allows Megumi to summon ten different shikigami using hand shadows. His summons include Divine Dogs, Nue, Toad, Great Serpent, and the powerful Mahoraga. He develops Domain Expansion: Chimera Shadow Garden, a non-lethal domain that enhances his shikigami.

Trivia

Megumi is the stepbrother of Tsumiki Fushiguro, whom he deeply cares for. His name means 'blessed' in Japanese. He is the son of Toji Fushiguro, the legendary Sorcerer Killer, inheriting his father's immense potential for jujutsu.
