Satoru Gojo

The Strongest Sorcerer

Affiliation: Tokyo Jujutsu High

Rank: Special Grade Sorcerer

Overview

Satoru Gojo is widely regarded as the strongest jujutsu sorcerer in the world. A teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High and the head of the Gojo Clan, he wields the inherited Limitless Technique and the Six Eyes. His ultimate goal is to reform the jujutsu world from within by raising a new generation of powerful sorcerers.

Appearance

Gojo is tall with snow-white hair and striking blue eyes that are always covered by his signature round sunglasses or a black blindfold. He has a lean, athletic build and wears a high-collared black coat with tailored black pants as part of his distinctive outfit.

Personality

Gojo is playful, cocky, and often comes across as unserious. He loves teasing his students and colleagues alike. However, beneath his carefree facade lies a deeply strategic and calculating individual who carries the immense burden of being the strongest.

Abilities

Gojo's Limitless Technique allows him to manipulate space at the atomic level. His Infinity creates an impenetrable barrier, his Cursed Technique Reversal: Red pushes matter away, and his Cursed Technique Amplification: Blue pulls matter in. His Domain Expansion: Unlimited Void traps enemies in infinite information, paralyzing them completely.

Trivia

Gojo's Six Eyes grant him near-perfect efficiency in cursed energy usage. He is so powerful that the higher-ups of the jujutsu world both rely on and fear him. He was sealed in the Prison Realm by Kenjaku using Geto's body, triggering the Shibuya Incident arc.
