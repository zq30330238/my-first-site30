Suguru Geto

Curse User Leader

Affiliation: Paranormal Liberation Front

Rank: Special Grade Sorcerer

Overview

Suguru Geto is a former student of Satoru Gojo and was once a respected Special Grade Sorcerer. After a series of traumatic events, he became disillusioned with protecting non-sorcerers and embraced an ideology of eradicating all humans who cannot see curses, becoming one of the most dangerous curse users in history.

Appearance

Geto has long black hair tied in a half-ponytail and narrow dark eyes. He has a tall, lean build and typically wears a Buddhist priest's robe-like outfit with a purple top and white pants. His calm expression belies his radical convictions.

Personality

Geto is calm, polite, and intellectually driven, even as he pursues genocide. He maintains an eerie serenity in all situations, believing his path is the only logical solution to the curse problem. His charisma allows him to attract powerful followers.

Abilities

Geto's Cursed Spirit Manipulation allows him to absorb, control, and summon curses of varying power levels. He commands thousands of cursed spirits, including Special Grade curses like Jogo, Hanami, Mahito, and Dagon. His technique also allows him to combine curses into more powerful forms.

Trivia

Geto was once Gojo's best friend, and their bond is central to both characters' development. His body is later taken over by Kenjaku, an ancient sorcerer who continues his plans. The movie Jujutsu Kaisen 0 revolves around the aftermath of his rampage.
