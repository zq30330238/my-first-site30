Ryomen Sukuna

King of Curses

Affiliation: None (Incarnated)

Rank: Special Grade Cursed Object

Overview

Ryomen Sukuna is the undisputed King of Curses, the most powerful cursed entity in existence. Over a thousand years ago, he was a human sorcerer of such immense power that he could not be killed. After his death, his soul was divided into twenty indestructible fingers, which became the ultimate cursed objects. He is currently incarnated within Yuji Itadori's body.

Appearance

Sukuna has four arms and two faces in his true form, with pink spiky hair like Yuji but with additional darkened features. His eyes are blood-red with multiple pupils. When possessing Yuji, cursed tattoos appear across the body, and his personality manifests through Yuji's features.

Personality

Sukuna is sadistic, arrogant, and utterly ruthless. He lives solely for his own pleasure and views humans as insignificant insects. He loves combat and seeks opponents who can entertain him. Despite his cruelty, he possesses a twisted code of honor and respects those who show unwavering conviction.

Abilities

Sukuna possesses an arsenal of devastating techniques including Cleave and Dismantle, which slice through anything. His Fire Arrow can incinerate entire areas. His Domain Expansion: Malevolent Shrine is unique for being a barrierless domain with a range of up to 200 meters, indiscriminately cutting everything within.

Trivia

Sukuna is sometimes depicted with four arms and two faces in his true form. In Japanese mythology, Ryomen Sukuna is a real figure from folklore. He is currently split across 20 fingers, of which 19 have been accounted for in the story.
