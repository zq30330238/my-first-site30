Toji Fushiguro

The Sorcerer Killer

Affiliation: None (Freelance Assassin)

Rank: Heavenly Restriction

Overview

Toji Fushiguro is the father of Megumi Fushiguro and a legendary figure known as the Sorcerer Killer. Born into the Zenin Clan with zero cursed energy due to a Heavenly Restriction, he instead developed unmatched physical abilities. He is feared throughout the jujutsu world as the man who can kill any sorcerer with pure physical prowess.

Appearance

Toji is tall with jet-black spiky hair and a chiseled, athletic build covered in scars from countless battles. He typically wears form-fitting black clothing that exposes his muscular arms and often carries multiple cursed tools. His sharp eyes convey a predator's instinct.

Personality

Toji is cold, pragmatic, and lives by his own code. He views himself as a tool rather than a person and takes jobs based on payment. Despite his ruthless reputation, he showed care for his son Megumi in his own way, leaving him to the Zenin Clan to potentially inherit their techniques.

Abilities

Toji's Heavenly Restriction grants him superhuman speed, strength, and senses at the cost of zero cursed energy. He carries an arsenal of cursed tools including the Inverted Spear of Heaven and the Chain of a Thousand Miles. He can kill Special Grade sorcerers through pure physical combat.

Trivia

Toji killed the previous Six Eyes user and defeated a younger Satoru Gojo in their first encounter. He sold Megumi to the Zenin Clan for a large sum. His return as a reanimated corpse during the Shibuya Incident showcases the continued impact of his legacy.
