Yuji Itadori

The Vessel of Sukuna

Affiliation: Tokyo Jujutsu High

Rank: Grade 1 Sorcerer

Overview

Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen. A high school student with extraordinary physical strength, he becomes the host of Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses, after swallowing a cursed finger to save his friends. He joins Tokyo Jujutsu High to learn sorcery and collect all of Sukuna's fingers before the King of Curses can regain full power.

Appearance

Yuji has spiky pink hair and brown eyes, giving him a distinctive and youthful appearance. He is of average height but possesses an incredibly muscular and athletic build from his days on the track team. He typically wears the standard Tokyo Jujutsu High uniform — a dark blue gakuran-style jacket with matching pants.

Personality

Yuji is kind-hearted, selfless, and deeply empathetic. He values human life above all else and carries the weight of Sukuna's potential destruction on his shoulders. Despite the darkness surrounding him, he maintains a cheerful disposition and a strong moral compass, believing that a good death comes from helping others.

Abilities

Yuji possesses superhuman physical abilities thanks to his innate talent and Sukuna's influence. He masters Divergent Fist, which delivers a delayed second impact, and learns Black Flash and Simple Domain. By swapping control with Sukuna, he gains access to devastating cursed techniques like Cleave and Dismantle.

Trivia

Yuji's favorite food is anything with protein, and he eats voraciously. His voice actors are Junya Enoki (Japanese) and Adam McArthur (English). He was created specifically to house Sukuna, making his existence a carefully orchestrated plan by higher-ups in the jujutsu world.
