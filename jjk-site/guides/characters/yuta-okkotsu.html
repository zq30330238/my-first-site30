Yuta Okkotsu

The Special Grade Prodigy

Affiliation: Tokyo Jujutsu High

Rank: Special Grade Sorcerer

Overview

Yuta Okkotsu is a Special Grade Sorcerer and the protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen 0. He was cursed by his childhood friend Rika Orimoto, who became a vengeful spirit of immense power after her death. Under Gojo's guidance, Yuta learns to control Rika's curse and becomes one of the most powerful sorcerers in the modern era.

Appearance

Yuta has short black hair and dark, tired eyes that reflect his troubled past. He has a lean, average build. He wears the standard Tokyo Jujutsu High uniform and carries a katana as a cursed tool. Dark rings under his eyes hint at the burden he carries.

Personality

Yuta is initially timid, guilt-ridden, and socially awkward, burdened by the deaths he caused through Rika. Through his training and friendships, he grows into a confident and caring sorcerer who values protecting others above all else. He carries himself with quiet determination.

Abilities

Yuta has immense cursed energy reserves, rivaled only by Gojo. He can channel Rika's power for devastating attacks and can copy other cursed techniques. His Domain Expansion creates a space where Rika can freely attack. He also masters Reverse Cursed Technique for healing.

Trivia

Yuta studied abroad in Africa before returning to Tokyo Jujutsu High. He is distantly related to the Gojo Clan. His relationship with Rika is both his greatest power and his deepest emotional wound.
