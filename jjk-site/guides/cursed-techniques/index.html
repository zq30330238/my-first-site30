Cursed Techniques

Explore the legendary cursed techniques of Jujutsu Kaisen — from inherited clan techniques to innate abilities born from unique circumstances.

Inherited

Limitless

User: Satoru Gojo

The Limitless is an inherited technique of the Gojo Clan that allows the user to manipulate space at the atomic level. It grants abilities including Infinity (impenetrable defense), Blue (attractive force), Red (repulsive force), and Hollow Purple (combined annihilation). Only those born with the Six Eyes can fully master it.

Abilities: Infinity, Blue, Red, Hollow Purple, Domain Expansion: Unlimited Void

Inherited

Ten Shadows

User: Megumi Fushiguro

The Ten Shadows Technique is an inherited technique of the Zenin Clan that allows the user to summon ten different shikigami using shadows. The shikigami include Divine Dogs, Nue, Toad, Great Serpent, Deer, and the immensely powerful Mahoraga. Users can also merge shikigami for stronger forms.

Abilities: Divine Dogs, Nue, Toad, Great Serpent, Mahoraga, Domain: Chimera Shadow Garden

Inherited

Cursed Speech

User: Toge Inumaki

Cursed Speech forces whatever the user says to become reality. Simple commands like 'stop' or 'sleep' affect targets directly, while 'die' can be lethal. Overuse damages the user's throat. The technique is inherited through the Inumaki bloodline and requires precise control to avoid cursing allies accidentally.

Abilities: Speech commands: Stop, Sleep, Explode, Die; Simple Domain

Innate

Boogie Woogie

User: Aoi Todo

Boogie Woogie allows the user to swap the positions of anything imbued with their cursed energy by clapping their hands together. This creates devastating tactical combinations in combat. The technique requires both hands to activate, which opponents can exploit by disabling one hand.

Abilities: Position swapping, tactical combos

Innate

Ratio Technique

User: Kento Nanami

The Ratio Technique divides any target into a 7:3 ratio line. When struck exactly at that line, the attack deals critical damage proportional to the target's cursed energy level. It is a precise and efficient technique that reflects Nanami's methodical personality.

Abilities: 7:3 Ratio line, critical hit

Innate

Cursed Spirit Manipulation

User: Suguru Geto

Cursed Spirit Manipulation allows the user to absorb, control, and summon cursed spirits of varying power levels. The user can command thousands of curses simultaneously and combine them into more powerful forms. Maximum Uzumaki compresses all absorbed curses into a single devastating beam attack.

Abilities: Curse absorption, summoning, combination, Maximum Uzumaki

Innate

Idle Transfiguration

User: Mahito

Idle Transfiguration allows the user to reshape the souls of themselves and others. This enables body manipulation, creation of Transfigured Humans, and self-healing by reforming the soul. The technique directly attacks the soul rather than the body, making it extremely difficult to defend against.

Abilities: Soul manipulation, Transfigured Humans, Self-Embodiment, Domain Expansion

Inherited (Kamo Clan)

Blood Manipulation

User: Choso

Blood Manipulation allows the user to control their own blood as a weapon. Techniques include Piercing Blood (high-speed blood projectiles), Blood Edge (blood blade), and Supernova (explosive blood scatter). Flowing Red Scale enhances physical abilities by altering blood composition.

Abilities: Piercing Blood, Blood Edge, Supernova, Flowing Red Scale

Innate

Construction

User: Mai Zenin

Construction allows the user to create matter from nothing using cursed energy. The complexity and size of the created object determine the cursed energy cost. While theoretically limitless, practical use is limited by the user's cursed energy reserves, restricting users to creating small objects.

Abilities: Materialization of objects from cursed energy

Advanced

Reverse Cursed Technique

User: Various

Reverse Cursed Technique multiplies negative cursed energy to create positive energy, which can heal injuries and neutralize curses. It is extremely difficult to master and requires precise cursed energy control. Yuta Okkotsu and Satoru Gojo are among the few who can use it effectively in combat.

Abilities: Healing, curse neutralization, RCT output
