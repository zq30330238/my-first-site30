Species: Human · Stand: Made in Heaven · Affiliation: Green Dolphin Street Prison, DIO's Disciple · First Appearance: Chapter 615 (1999)

Overview

Enrico Pucci is the main antagonist of Stone Ocean, the sixth part of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure. A Catholic priest and devoted disciple of DIO, Pucci seeks to achieve 'Heaven' by following DIO's instructions. His manipulation of the Joestar bloodline and his role in orchestrating Jolyne's imprisonment set the events of Stone Ocean in motion. Pucci's calm demeanor and religious conviction make him a unique and terrifying villain.

Appearance

Pucci is a tall, dark-skinned man with short white hair and a calm, serene expression. He wears priestly robes with a distinctive cross necklace. His eyes are sharp and intelligent, often half-lidded with an air of superiority. Pucci has an athletic build beneath his robes. His appearance becomes more ethereal as his Stand evolves.

Personality

Pucci is calm, patient, and utterly convinced of the righteousness of his mission. He views himself as a servant of fate, using his faith to justify his actions. Pucci genuinely believes achieving Heaven will benefit humanity, making him a sympathetic villain. He is calculating and patient, willing to wait for decades to accomplish his goals. His devotion to DIO borders on religious worship.

Abilities

Pucci's Stand evolves multiple times. Whitesnake can extract Stands and memories from people, storing them as discs. C-Moon manipulates gravity within a range. Made in Heaven accelerates time, eventually causing the universe to reset. Pucci can perceive events during accelerated time while others cannot react. His abilities are directly tied to his pursuit of Heaven.

Trivia

Pucci's Stands are named after songs by David Bowie and Jimmy Page. He has six brothers, one of whom, Weather Report, is a major character in Stone Ocean. Pucci's plan to achieve Heaven involves 14 specific phrases. He is the only antagonist whose actions permanently change the JoJo universe.
