Species: Human · Stand: Sex Pistols · Affiliation: Passione, Bucciarati's Team · First Appearance: Chapter 461 (1995)

Overview

Guido Mista is a member of Bucciarati's team in Golden Wind. A sharp-shooting Stand user with a paranoid personality, Mista serves as the team's ranged combat specialist. His Stand Sex Pistols allows him to redirect bullets mid-flight with incredible precision. Despite his constant anxiety about the number four, Mista is a reliable and courageous fighter who would do anything for his teammates.

Appearance

Mista has dark hair styled in dreadlocks, often partially covered by a striped cap with chin straps. He wears a cropped vest with star patterns and tight pants. Mista carries a revolver as his weapon of choice. His outfit mixes street style with cowboy influences, complete with gloves and distinctive shoes. He has a lean, athletic build.

Personality

Mista is perpetually anxious, superstitious, and prone to panic, especially regarding the number four, which sounds like 'death' in Japanese. Despite this, he is fiercely loyal to Bucciarati and his teammates. Mista is brave in battle and never abandons a friend. He has a strong sense of justice and particularly hates those who harm innocent people. His humor and neurotic tendencies provide comic relief.

Abilities

Sex Pistols consists of six bullet-like entities that Mista can command to redirect his fired bullets mid-flight. Each entity has its own personality and can interact with objects. Mista can use them to create curved shots, ricochets, and precise multi-angle attacks. He is a skilled marksman even without his Stand. His tactical use of cover and angles makes him deadly in ranged combat.

Trivia

Mista's Stand is named after the Sex Pistols punk band. He refuses to use anything associated with the number four and will count 1-2-3-5-6. Mista carries six bullets because he fears even numbers. His hat with chin straps is one of the most distinctive designs in JoJo's.
