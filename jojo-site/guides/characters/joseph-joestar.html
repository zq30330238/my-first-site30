Species: Human · Stand: Hermit Purple · Affiliation: Speedwagon Foundation, Stardust Crusaders · First Appearance: Chapter 45 (1987)

Overview

Joseph Joestar, the grandson of Jonathan Joestar, is the protagonist of Battle Tendency and a major character in Stardust Crusaders. Known for his clever tactics, playful personality, and incredible Hamon abilities, Joseph battles the ancient Pillar Men to prevent them from conquering humanity. His resourcefulness and trickster nature set him apart from other Joestars, often winning battles through wit rather than raw power.

Appearance

Young Joseph is tall and muscular with dark hair and a confident grin. He typically wears a distinctive outfit with a headband, open vest, and scarf. In his older appearance during Part 3, Joseph has gray hair and a more weathered look, wearing a suit with a cape. His design changes significantly with age but retains his signature confident posture.

Personality

Joseph is charismatic, witty, and endlessly resourceful. Unlike his straightforward grandfather, Joseph relies on trickery, prediction, and adaptability in combat. He frequently taunts his opponents, uses clever traps, and thinks several steps ahead. Despite his playful demeanor, Joseph deeply cares for his friends and family. He shows remarkable courage against overwhelming odds and never gives up, no matter how dire the situation becomes.

Abilities

Joseph is a master of Hamon, the life energy martial art used to combat vampires and Pillar Men. He develops the ability to use Hamon through various creative applications. His Stand Hermit Purple manifests as thorny vines that can capture spirits and take psychic photographs. Joseph's true genius lies in his tactical mind and ability to predict his opponent's moves, famously claiming to know what they will say next.

Trivia

Joseph is the only Joestar to appear as both a protagonist and a supporting character across multiple parts. His 'Your next line is' prediction trick is one of the series' most iconic running gags. Joseph ages from 18 in Part 2 to 67 in Part 3. He is a skilled pilot, mechanic, and con artist.
