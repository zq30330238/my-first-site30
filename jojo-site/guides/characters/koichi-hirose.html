Species: Human · Stand: Echoes · Affiliation: Morioh, Josuke's Friend · First Appearance: Chapter 267 (1992)

Overview

Koichi Hirose is a major supporting character in Diamond is Unbreakable who also appears in Golden Wind. Starting as a timid and insecure high school student, Koichi develops into a confident Stand user through his adventures with Josuke. His Stand Echoes evolves through three distinct forms, each with unique abilities. Koichi serves as the heart and narrator of Part 4, representing personal growth and courage.

Appearance

Koichi is short and has a youthful face with hair styled in an almost bowl-like cut. He wears his school uniform neatly. Koichi's appearance reflects his young age and developing confidence. As the series progresses, he carries himself with more assurance. His small stature is often commented on by other characters.

Personality

Koichi starts as timid and insecure, easily intimidated by delinquents like Josuke. However, he possesses incredible inner strength and moral courage. Koichi is kind, thoughtful, and loyal to his friends. He grows significantly throughout the story, becoming a brave Stand user who stands up to powerful enemies. His relationship with Yukako Yamagishi adds complexity to his character.

Abilities

Echoes evolves through three Acts. Act 1 creates sound effects that affect reality. Act 2 embeds sound effects into objects with programmable effects. Act 3 is a short-range Stand that can apply immense gravitational force through the word 'Three Freeze.' Each Act represents Koichi's growth and increasing combat capabilities.

Trivia

Koichi's Stand Echoes is named after the band Echoes. He narrates most of Diamond is Unbreakable, making him the viewpoint character. Koichi appears in Golden Wind as a brief ally to Giorno. His height is approximately 157 cm (5'2"), making him one of the shortest JoJo characters.
