Species: Human · Stand: The Hand · Affiliation: Morioh, Josuke's Friend · First Appearance: Chapter 269 (1992)

Overview

Okuyasu Nijimura is a major supporting character in Diamond is Unbreakable. Initially an antagonist controlled by his father's Stand, Okuyasu quickly becomes one of Josuke's closest friends. Despite his simple-minded nature and poor decision-making, Okuyasu's loyalty and kind heart make him an invaluable ally. His Stand The Hand has one of the most dangerous abilities in the series, which he uses with surprising creativity.

Appearance

Okuyasu has a wild hairstyle with two distinctive points sticking up. He wears a modified school uniform with a fur-lined jacket and gloves. His build is athletic and slightly bulky. Okuyasu has a rough, delinquent appearance with a perpetually confused expression. His outfit is decorated with zippers and metal accents.

Personality

Okuyasu is kind-hearted but not particularly bright, often acting before thinking. He is fiercely loyal to Josuke and their friends, viewing them as brothers. Okuyasu wears his heart on his sleeve, expressing joy, anger, and sadness openly. He respects his older brother Keicho deeply despite their different approaches to life. His innocence and straightforwardness often provide comic relief.

Abilities

The Hand has a right hand that can erase anything it swipes, including space itself. By swiping through the air, Okuyasu can instantly transport objects or people by erasing the space between them. The Hand's erasure power is incredibly dangerous, capable of killing enemies in a single strike. Okuyasu's lack of intelligence limits his use of this powerful Stand.

Trivia

Okuyasu's Stand is named after the song 'The Hand' by The J. Geils Band. He and his brother Keicho were mistreated by their Stand-using father. Okuyasu is the second character to join Josuke's group. His English voice actor has said Okuyasu is the most fun character to voice.
