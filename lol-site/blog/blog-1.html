Jhin: The Virtuoso - Mastering the Fourth Shot

March 25, 2025 · 5 min read

Understanding Jhin's Unique Attack Rhythm

Jhin's attack speed is fixed, but every fourth shot crits for massive damage based on missing health. This mechanic forces you to pace your damage. Weave in W and Q between autos to maximize DPS without wasting the fourth shot. Reloading is your window of vulnerability--use it to reposition or deploy traps.

Always count your shots. The fourth shot is your biggest damage, so hold it for high-value targets like carries or low-health tanks. A well-timed fourth shot can secure a kill before the enemy reacts.

Laning phase is about trading with your fourth shot. Use Q to last-hit and poke simultaneously. Your W roots enemies marked by traps, ally CC, or your ult. Land a W after your fourth shot to keep pressure. Punish enemies that stand near low-health minions--Q bounce damage plus a follow-up auto can chunk them hard.

Positioning and Trap Placement

Jhin has no dash, so positioning is everything. Place traps (E) in brushes, jungle choke points, and behind tower to spot flanks. Traps slow and mark enemies, setting up your W from long range. In mid-game, trap objectives like Dragon pit entrances before a fight. Use your ult to zone or pick off retreating foes--each shot slows, and the fourth shot crits for max damage.

Tips for trap placement: Put one trap in the lane's side brush to deny enemy vision control. Stack two traps in a narrow path to guarantee a root if an enemy steps on them. In teamfights, drop a trap under yourself to deter divers. Remember, traps last 2 minutes--prep key areas early.

Teamfight Execution

Stay at max auto-attack range and use your fourth shot on priority targets. Never frontline--Jhin dies fast under focus. Start fights with your ultimate to soften enemies or force summoners. After enemies commit, kite with autos and W roots. Your passive movespeed from crits helps reposition. Use Galeforce or Flash to dodge key abilities, then land a finishing fourth shot.

Itemization matters: Collector works with your fourth shot execute. Infinity Edge boosts crit damage. RFC extends your range for safer fourth shots. Against tanks, build Lord Dominik's Regards. Against burst, consider Shieldbow or GA. Adapt your build to the enemy team's threats.

Jhin excels in pick compositions. Pair him with heavy engage supports like Leona or Nautilus. Their CC sets up your W root and ult. Avoid comps that need sustained DPS--Jhin is a burst/control mage hybrid. Coordinate with jungler for early dives: your fourth shot plus W can lock down enemies under tower.

Practice the fourth shot timing in practice tool. Once it feels second nature, you'll climb faster. Jhin rewards patience and precision, not spray-and-pray. Respect your reload, disrespect no enemy cooldown.
