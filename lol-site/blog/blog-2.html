Aurelion Sol: The Star Forger Character Analysis

April 1, 2025 · 5 min read

Understanding Aurelion Sol's Unique Playstyle

Aurelion Sol is a control mage who excels at map-wide pressure and kiting. Unlike most mid laners, his damage revolves around orbiting stars that automatically strike enemies hit by his passive. This forces you to constantly reposition to keep enemies inside the star's path. His strength lies in his ability to roam with E - Comet of Legend and zone enemies with Q - Starsurge. However, his immobility and vulnerability to assassins make positioning critical.

To maximize effectiveness, you must learn to orbit at maximum range while using W - Celestial Expansion to extend the star radius. Timing this ability is key--using it during a skirmish allows you to hit multiple targets while staying safe. Many new players underestimate the importance of his passive damage. Every star auto-attack matters, especially early game where each hit can deny last hits and chip down the enemy.

Pro Tip: Use your E - Comet of Legend to roam bot lane immediately after hitting level 6. A successful gank can snowball your team's lead. Remember to keep your W - Celestial Expansion active only while auto-attacking to conserve mana.

Mastering Stardust and E - Comet of Legend

Stardust is Aurelion Sol's resource system after his rework. You generate Stardust by hitting champions with stars, and spending it to empower Q or to end your W early for a cooldown refund. Efficient Stardust management separates average players from great ones. Use Q - Starsurge to stun priority targets during ganks or to set up picks. The ability grows in radius as it travels, so leading your target is essential.

E - Comet of Legend is your roaming tool. It allows you to fly long distances over terrain, but you cannot cast spells or attack during flight. Use it to rotate to sidelanes, to flank enemies, or to escape unfavorable fights. A common mistake is using E too early in the lane phase. Save it for when you have shoved the wave or seen the enemy jungler on the opposite side of the map. Combining E with Q allows you to land a massive stun upon arrival.

Placement of your ult, R - The Skies Descend, can disengage or finish low health foes. Use it to peel for yourself when assassins dive, or to zone multiple enemies during dragon fights.

Positioning and Teamfight Execution

In teamfights, Aurelion Sol stays at the edge of the fight. You are not a frontliner. Your job is to poke with stars and look for an opportunity to land a multi-man stun with Q. Always keep moving--standing still makes you an easy target for hooks or burst. Use W - Celestial Expansion to increase your damage output, but only when you can safely hit enemies without overextending.

Fight around objectives. Your ult can disrupt enemy formation and secure kills. If you fall behind, you can still be useful by clearing waves with your stars and providing cross-map presence with E. Build items like Rylai's Crystal Scepter to slow enemies and make landing stars easier. Liandry's Anguish provides additional burn against tanks. Remember, your damage scales with how long you can keep enemies inside your star orbit.

Against assassins, stay with your team and hold your Q for peel. Use R to knock back anyone who dives you. With practice, Aurelion Sol becomes a devastating control mage who dictates the flow of the game.
