Rengar: The Pridestalker - Tips for One-Shotting Carries

March 28, 2025 · 6 min read

Understanding Rengar's Core Mechanics

Rengar thrives on bush control and ferocity management. His passive allows him to leap to enemies from bushes, granting bonus attack speed and a critical strike. This leap is your primary gap-closer and burst tool. Always maintain vision of nearby brush in lane or jungle. Use the Ctrl+1 taunt to check if enemies are in fog--it triggers a roar that reveals sound effects if they're close. Stack ferocity to 5 points before engaging; an empowered ability determines your kill potential. Empowered Q (Savagery) offers the highest single-target burst, while empowered E (Bola Strike) roots enemies--ideal for locking down slippery targets.

Optimal Combo for Deleting a Carry

Start by charging your ferocity to 4 stacks on jungle camps or minions. Activate R (Thrill of the Hunt) to reveal the nearest enemy champion. Jump from stealth with your passive, landing an auto-attack. Immediately use Q (Savagery) to reset auto, then follow with W (Battle Roar) for AoE damage and healing, then E (Bola Strike). Use empowered Q if you have 5 ferocity, or empowered E if you need CC. Finish with the second W if you took the double-cast mechanic from the W. This combo kills most ADCs before they react, especially with Duskblade of Draktharr or Ghostblade.

Pro tip: Use your control wards to deny enemy vision of bushes. When you ult, enemy champions see an exclamation mark only if they have sight of you. Stand in a bush before ulting to make the first jump unexpected. Pair Rengar with a support like Ivern or Lulu for easier ganks.

Itemization and Rune Choices

Domination primary with Electrocute for burst. Take Sudden Impact, Eyeball Collection, and Relentless Hunter. Secondary Precision with Triumph and Legend: Alacrity. For items, rush Duskblade of Draktharr for invisibility resets, then Youmuu's Ghostblade or Edge of Night. Against heavy armor, build Lord Dominik's Regards. Situational: Guardian Angel or Mercurial Scimitar. Avoid only building crit--Rengar's Q no longer scales with crit chance. Focus on lethality and ability haste.
