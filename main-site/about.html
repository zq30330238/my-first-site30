About JYCSD

Jiangyin CheSuDi — Built to share knowledge that improves everyday life

Our Story

JYCSD stands for Jiangyin CheSuDi, founded in Jiangyin, China. What started as a single healthy eating blog has grown into a network of expert-curated lifestyle websites covering the topics that matter most to people worldwide.

Our mission is simple: provide free, high-quality, evidence-based information that helps people make better decisions in their daily lives — whether that's choosing what to eat, how to care for a pet, managing money, or planning a trip.

Our Network

JYCSD operates six dedicated content websites, each focused on a specific area of interest:

  • HealthyEats — Nutrition, recipes, and meal planning
  • PetCare Hub — Pet health, behavior, and care guides
  • HomeJoy — Home improvement, gardening, and DIY
  • MoneyWise — Personal finance, investing, and budgeting
  • TechNest — Technology reviews, guides, and tips
  • TripRoute — Travel guides, tips, and destination inspiration

Each site is independently maintained with its own content team, ensuring depth and expertise in every topic area.

Our Commitment

Accuracy

Every article is fact-checked and reviewed by editors with relevant subject matter expertise. We cite credible sources and correct errors promptly.

Transparency

We're open about how we operate. Our content is free for everyone, supported by advertising. We clearly distinguish between editorial content and advertisements.

Accessibility

All our content is free, loads fast on any device, and is written in clear, straightforward language that anyone can understand.

Independence

Our editorial decisions are made independently of our advertising partners. We recommend what we genuinely believe is best for our readers.
