What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and function efficiently.

How We Use Cookies

Across our network, we use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality like navigation.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our websites (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to deliver relevant advertisements.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices for a personalized experience.

Google AdSense Cookies

Google uses the DoubleClick cookie to serve interest-based ads across our network. You can opt out at Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Consent

By continuing to use our websites, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
