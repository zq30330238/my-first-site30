About MineGuide

MineGuide is the most comprehensive independent Minecraft guide resource on the web — built by players, for players.

We cover every aspect of Minecraft with detailed, step-by-step guides: crafting recipes, mob strategies, building tutorials, redstone engineering, enchanting combinations, and biome exploration. Our team of dedicated Minecraft players tests every strategy in survival mode before publishing.

What Makes Us Different

  • Every guide tested in vanilla survival Minecraft
  • Updated within 48 hours of each Minecraft update
  • Clean, ad-supported model — no paywalls, no forced signups
  • Mobile-optimized for reading while playing
