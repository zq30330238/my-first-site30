Ender Dragon Fight Strategy: Preparation and Combat Tactics

March 28, 2025 · 5 min read

Preparing for the Fight

Before you step into the End, gear up properly. Wear at least diamond armor with full enchantments. Prioritize Protection IV on all pieces, plus Blast Protection on your chestplate to reduce crystal explosion damage. A netherite set is ideal but not required. Bring a bow with Power V and Infinity or a good supply of arrows. A sword with Sharpness V and Looting III helps with endermen and the dragon. Enchant your pickaxe with Efficiency V and Silk Touch to collect end crystals quickly.

Potions are critical. Brew Slow Falling potions to prevent fall damage from the dragon's knockback. Fire Resistance potions protect against the dragon's breath attack. A Strength II potion boosts damage output, and Healing II potions keep you alive. Pack at least a stack of golden apples and one enchanted golden apple for emergencies. Bring several beds or end crystals--beds explode when you use them in the End, dealing massive damage to the dragon.

Blockquote tip:

Place beds near the exit portal pillars to damage the dragon multiple times before the final phase. Each bed explosion deals 50% of the dragon's health but requires precise timing.

The Battle: Tactics and Tips

Enter the End and immediately locate the end crystals on the obsidian pillars. Each healing crystal regenerates the dragon's health. Destroy them first. Use your bow to shoot the crystal on the caged pillars, or place blocks to get a clear shot. For the iron-bar cages, break them with your pickaxe. If the crystal is on an open pillar, you can shoot it easily.

Once all crystals are down, focus on the dragon. Time your attacks between its charges. Stand on the ground and shoot the dragon with your bow when it dives at you. Use a crossbow with Piercing if you prefer. When the dragon lands on the portal, run in and hit it with your sword. Pop a Strength potion for extra damage. Avoid the dragon's breath--move away when you see purple particles.

Use the end gateway to return to the central island if you fall off. Bring a water bucket to break falls. If you have Slow Falling, drink it before the dragon knocks you. Watch out for endermen; don't look at them, or wear a pumpkin on your head (it blocks line of sight but reduces visibility). A better method is to use a carving pumpkin just for the eyes, but a normal pumpkin works in a pinch.

When the dragon perches, you can also place beds near it. Run away before the explosion to avoid damage. This tactic speeds up the fight significantly. After the dragon dies, collect the Dragon Egg and the portal opens, giving you 20 levels of XP. Return to the Overworld through the gateway or kill the ended dragon for elytra in the end ships.
