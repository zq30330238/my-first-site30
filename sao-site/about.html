About SAO Wiki

Welcome to the Sword Art Online Wiki, your ultimate resource for everything related to the SAO universe. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, accurate, and well-organized information about the characters, weapons, story arcs, and worlds of Sword Art Online.

Founded in 2022, the SAO Wiki is maintained by a dedicated team of anime enthusiasts who are passionate about the series created by Reki Kawahara. We strive to create the most complete reference guides available online, covering every aspect of the SAO universe from Aincrad to Underworld.

Our content is carefully researched and written to be accessible to both newcomers and longtime fans. Whether you are looking for detailed character stats, weapon specifications, or a complete timeline of events, you will find what you need here.

What We Cover

  • Complete character profiles for all major SAO characters
  • Weapon database covering legendary swords, firearms, and Divine Objects
  • Detailed story arc breakdowns from Aincrad to the War of Underworld
  • Comprehensive guides to the Aincrad floors and their unique features
  • Information about VR worlds including ALfheim Online, Gun Gale Online, and Underworld

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing high-quality content that respects the original source material. All character and story information is based on the light novels, anime adaptations, and official sources. We do not fabricate facts or invent statistics.

If you have questions, suggestions, or corrections, please contact us through our contact page. We value community input and continuously improve our content based on reader feedback.
