Arc Details

Aincrad Arc

Episodes 1-14

The Aincrad arc is where everything begins. 10,000 players are trapped in Sword Art Online by Akihiko Kayaba on November 6, 2022. The only way to escape is to clear all 100 floors of Aincrad, but death in the game means death in real life. This arc follows Kirito, Asuna, and the front-line players as they fight for survival, fall in love, and ultimately face the creator of their prison. Key moments include Kirito and Asuna's marriage, the death of the Moonlit Black Cats guild, and the final duel with Heathcliff on the 75th floor.

Fairy Dance Arc

Episodes 15-25

After SAO, Kirito discovers that Asuna is still trapped — this time in Alfheim Online under the control of the corrupt researcher Sugou Nobuyuki. To save her, Kirito dives into ALO as a Spriggan and learns to fly. Along the way, he reunites with his cousin Suguha (Leafa) and discovers the truth about Sugou's cruel experiments. The arc climaxes in an epic aerial battle atop the World Tree, where Kirito must overcome both Sugou's sadistic cruelty and the game's impossible defenses.

Phantom Bullet Arc

Episodes 1-14 (Season 2)

Kirito is recruited by Kikuoka to investigate a series of mysterious deaths linked to Gun Gale Online, where a player known as Death Gun claims to kill people both in-game and in real life. Entering GGO, Kirito befriends the sniper Sinon and helps her confront her own trauma while hunting down the killer during the Bullet of Bullets tournament. This arc explores themes of PTSD, the nature of death, and the blurring lines between virtual and real violence.

Mother's Rosario Arc

Episodes 18-24 (Season 2)

Asuna takes center stage in this heartfelt arc. She befriends Yuuki Konno, the strongest player in ALO known as Zekken (Absolute Sword). Together they search for Yuuki's former guild members and uncover the truth about her terminal illness. Yuuki, a young girl living her final days with the help of the Medicuboid, teaches Asuna about courage, friendship, and living life to the fullest. The arc's emotional climax revolves around Yuuki's last battle and the inheritance of Mother's Rosario.

Alicization Arc

Episodes 1-47 (Season 3)

The longest and most ambitious SAO arc begins when Kirito is attacked with a lethal drug and his soul is downloaded into the massive-scale Underworld. Here, he meets Eugeo and Alice, and together they journey toward the Central Cathedral. This arc explores the nature of artificial souls, the boundaries between AI and human consciousness, and the ethics of creating virtual people. Featuring epic battles, deep world-building, and complex philosophical questions, Alicization represents the pinnacle of the SAO series.

War of Underworld Arc

Episodes 24-47 (Season 3 Part 2)

The War of Underworld is the climactic battle between the Human Empire and the Dark Territory, manipulated by the real-world American players corporation. As Kirito lies catatonic from the trauma of his battle with Eugeo, Asuna, Sinon, Leafa, and the entire SAO survivor army dive into Underworld to defend it. This arc features massive-scale warfare, the return of Kirito at his most powerful moment, and the conclusion of the Alicization saga. The battle for the future of AI life reaches its epic conclusion.
