Title: Integrity Knight / Alice Synthesis Thirty · Series: Sword Art Online

Appearance

Alice is a tall, beautiful young woman with long golden-blonde hair and striking blue eyes. Her Integrity Knight armor is elaborate white and gold, featuring plate armor with decorative engravings and a flowing cape. She wears a distinctive metal crown-like headpiece. Her sword Fragrant Olive is a longsword with a golden blade that glows with sacred power. In casual settings, she wears elegant white dresses befitting her knightly status. Her bearing is always regal and composed.

Personality

Alice is disciplined, principled, and unwavering in her sense of justice — at least initially. After breaking free from the Axiom Church's brainwashing, she retains her strong moral compass but applies it more humanely. She is fiercely protective of her friends, especially Kirito and the younger Selka. She struggles with guilt over actions taken while under the Taboo Index's control. Her leadership instincts are natural, and she commands respect through competence rather than authority.

Abilities & Combat Style

Alice wields Fragrant Olive with masterful swordsmanship, capable of releasing Sacred Arts-enhanced slashes. As an Integrity Knight, she has extremely high Priority in the Underworld system, giving her enhanced physical abilities. She can use self-healing Sacred Arts. After training under Kirito and Eugeo, she develops original sword techniques that blend Integrity Knight training with personal creativity. Her Release Recollection is one of the strongest in Underworld.

Trivia

Alice's title 'Synthesis Thirty' indicates she was the 30th Integrity Knight created. The original Alice Zuberg (before synthesis) was the childhood friend of Kirito and Eugeo. She was named after the Alice in Wonderland books that existed in the real world. She becomes the ruler of Underworld after the war. Her bond with Kirito and Asuna is described as a unique 'family' relationship.
