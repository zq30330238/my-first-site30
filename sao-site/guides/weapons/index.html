SAO Weapons

Explore the legendary weapons of the Sword Art Online universe — from Kirito's iconic swords to Sinon's sniper rifle and Alice's Divine Object.

Weapon Details

Elucidator

Legendary Sword Wielder: Kirito

Elucidator is Kirito's signature black longsword and one of the most powerful weapons in Aincrad. It was obtained as a drop from the 50th Floor Boss, making it a legendary-class weapon with exceptional attack power. The blade is entirely black with a sleek, elegant design that perfectly complements Kirito's Black Swordsman persona. When combined with Dark Repulser, Kirito's secondary sword, Elucidator forms the foundation of his devastating Dual Blades fighting style. Its rarity and power made it one of the most coveted weapons among front-line players, though none could use it as effectively as Kirito.

Appearance & Design

Elucidator has a 90 cm jet-black blade with a subtle silver edge that gleams when catching light. The hilt is wrapped in black leather with a silver crossguard featuring minimal ornamentation. Its scabbard is equally black with silver fittings. The sword's minimalist design emphasizes function over aesthetics, though its very simplicity makes it striking.

Dark Repulser

Divine-Class Sword Wielder: Kirito

Dark Repulser is Kirito's divine-class longsword, crafted specifically for him by Lisbeth using the rare material 'Crystallite Ingot.' The sword is a brilliant silver-white color with a pale blue aura — the perfect contrast to the black Elucidator. It was created during Kirito and Lisbeth's journey to obtain the legendary materials, a quest that forged their lasting friendship. Dark Repulser's divine classification made it one of the highest-grade weapons in SAO at the time of its creation, capable of matching the best boss drop weapons. Together with Elucidator, it formed Kirito's dual-wielding arsenal.

Appearance & Design

Dark Repulser features a 95 cm blade of brilliant silver-white metal that emits a faint blue luminescence. The hilt is wrapped in white leather with a silver crossguard adorned with small blue crystals. The blade appears almost ethereal, with a mirror-like finish that reflects light beautifully. Its scabbard is white with blue accents.

Hecate II

Anti-Material Sniper Rifle Wielder: Sinon

The PGM Hecate II is Sinon's legendary sniper rifle and one of the most powerful firearms in Gun Gale Online. This French-made anti-material rifle fires .50 BMG rounds with devastating accuracy at extreme ranges. Sinon's mastery of the Hecate II earned her a place among GGO's elite players and made her a feared competitor in the Bullet of Bullets tournaments. The weapon's incredible power comes with significant drawbacks — its weight slows movement and its long reload time leaves the user vulnerable. Sinon's ability to overcome these limitations through pure skill is what makes her the top sniper in GGO.

Appearance & Design

The Hecate II is a massive bullpup sniper rifle with a long barrel and integrated bipod. Its color scheme is olive drab and black with a large scope mounted on top. The rifle is noticeably larger than Sinon herself, creating an iconic visual contrast between the petite sniper and her oversized weapon. Its design emphasizes stopping power over portability.

Fragrant Olive

Divine Object / Sacred Sword Wielder: Alice

Fragrant Olive is the sacred sword wielded by Alice Synthesis Thirty, classified as a Divine Object — the highest grade of weapon in Underworld. The golden blade was created using ancient secret techniques and is one of the most powerful weapons in the Underworld. Its name comes from the olive tree's symbolism of peace and the fragrant blossoms that characterized Alice's hometown of Rulid Village. When its full power is released through Release Recollection, Fragrant Olive can create devastating energy attacks that rival the strongest Sacred Arts.

Appearance & Design

Fragrant Olive has a 100 cm golden blade that seems to glow from within, with intricate engravings along its length depicting scenes from Sacred Arts lore. The hilt is wrapped in white leather with a gold crossguard featuring olive branch motifs. The entire weapon radiates a warm golden light when activated, creating an aura of divine power.

Mother's Rosario

Original Sword Skill Wielder: Yuuki Konno

Mother's Rosario is the legendary Original Sword Skill created by Yuuki Konno, known as the Zekken (Absolute Sword). This eleven-hit combo was considered the fastest and most powerful sword skill in Alfheim Online, a testament to Yuuki's extraordinary talent. The skill's name honors Yuuki's deceased mother, who passed away before her. Mother's Rosario became Asuna's most treasured inherited technique after Yuuki taught it to her. Its legacy extends beyond combat — it represents Yuuki's determination to live fully despite her terminal illness and her desire to leave something meaningful behind.

Appearance & Design

Mother's Rosario manifests as a series of eleven rapid sword strikes executed in a flowing, dance-like pattern. Each strike connects seamlessly into the next, creating an unbroken chain of damage. When used, the user's blade glows with a soft pink light, and afterimages of each strike remain visible momentarily. The final strike is a powerful thrust that delivers the highest damage of the combo.

Holy Sword of the Knights of Blood

Unique Skill / Administrator Sword Wielder: Heathcliff

The Holy Sword was the unique skill wielded by Heathcliff, leader of the Knights of the Blood Oath. Like Kirito's Dual Blades, it was a system-exclusive skill that could not be obtained through normal gameplay — instead it was granted to Heathcliff as the game master administrator. The Holy Sword skill granted unparalleled defensive capabilities, making Heathcliff virtually unbeatable in combat. Combined with his Immortal Object status, it made him the final boss of Aincrad itself. The skill's true nature as Kayaba's curator tool was hidden from all players until the final confrontation.

Appearance & Design

The Holy Sword takes the form of a medium-length longsword with a blade that emits a soft white-gold glow. It has an ornate hilt with crimson and gold details matching Heathcliff's armor. When used, the sword creates defensive energy barriers that can block even the strongest attacks. Its design is both beautiful and imposing, fitting its wielder's status as the strongest player in SAO.
