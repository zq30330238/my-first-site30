Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, enhance the user experience, and provide information to website owners about how the site is being used. Cookies cannot execute code or access other information on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of our website. They enable core features such as navigation and access to secure areas. The site cannot function properly without these cookies. Essential cookies do not store any personally identifiable information and are typically session-based, meaning they expire when you close your browser.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies, including those from Google Analytics, to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect information such as which pages are visited most frequently, how users navigate between pages, and how long they stay on each page. This data is aggregated and anonymous. It helps us improve our content and user experience. Analytics cookies may persist for up to 26 months.

Advertising Cookies

Glow Guide uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners use advertising cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to build a profile of your interests based on the websites you visit. This enables them to show ads that are more relevant to you across the web. These cookies track visits to our site and other sites, but they do not directly identify you personally.

Google AdSense and DoubleClick

Google AdSense uses the DoubleClick cookie to serve interest-based advertisements. When you visit a website that uses AdSense, Google may place a cookie in your browser. This cookie allows Google to recognize your browser when you visit other sites in the Google Display Network. You can learn more about how Google uses cookies in advertising at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting adssettings.google.com. Opting out does not mean you will see fewer ads; it means the ads you see will be less relevant to your interests.

Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can choose to block all cookies, accept only first-party cookies, or delete specific cookies. Below are links to instructions for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Block all cookies
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data

Note that disabling all cookies may affect the functionality of this and many other websites. Some features may not work as intended if cookies are blocked.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to the cookies set by Glow Guide, third-party services we use (such as Google AdSense and Google Analytics) may set their own cookies. These third-party cookies are governed by the privacy policies of the respective providers. We do not have direct control over these cookies, but you can manage or opt out of them through the tools described above.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
