Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Glow Guide (operated by Myers Media) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at beauty.jycsd.com. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access the site.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you contact us via email. This may include your name, email address, and any other information you choose to include in your message.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit Glow Guide, certain information is collected automatically by our analytics and advertising partners. This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and the date and time of your visit. This information is aggregated and used for analytical purposes to improve our content and user experience.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and serve relevant advertisements. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and messages
  • To analyze site usage and improve our content, layout, and user experience
  • To display personalized advertising through Google AdSense and other ad networks
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activity
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking

Glow Guide uses Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service, to display ads. Google uses cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to serve ads based on your previous visits to our site and other websites across the internet. You may opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting Google's Ads Settings at adssettings.google.com.

We also use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect anonymous traffic data. These services use cookies to help us understand how visitors interact with our site. The data collected is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services on Glow Guide:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Analytics: For analyzing site traffic and user behavior. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery and security. Cloudflare's privacy policy is available at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

These third-party service providers have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information. We encourage you to review their policies.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Email inquiries are stored for a reasonable period to allow us to respond to your inquiry and for record-keeping purposes. Analytics data is retained in aggregated form for up to 26 months.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit the processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data for marketing or analytics purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@jycsd.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Glow Guide is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
