About HomeJoy

Welcome to HomeJoy, a home and garden resource created for real people living in real homes. We believe that a happier home doesn't require a massive renovation budget, a sprawling backyard, or a degree in interior design. It requires practical knowledge, a little inspiration, and the confidence to try something new.

What We Do

HomeJoy publishes well-researched, approachable guides on four core topics:

  • DIY Home Improvement — Step-by-step projects that anyone can tackle, with honest assessments of cost, difficulty, and time required.
  • Indoor and Small-Space Gardening — From keeping a snake plant alive to growing cherry tomatoes on a balcony, we cover the full spectrum of plant parenthood.
  • Home Organization and Decor — Decluttering strategies, storage solutions, and simple design ideas that make rooms feel bigger, brighter, and more peaceful.
  • Seasonal Home Maintenance — Checklists and guides that help you protect your biggest investment, one season at a time.

Our Approach

Every article on HomeJoy is written with three principles in mind: practicality (advice you can actually use this weekend), clarity (no jargon, no fluff), and honesty (when a project is hard, we tell you; when it's cheaper to hire a pro, we say so). We test techniques in real homes, research thoroughly, and update our content regularly to keep it accurate.
