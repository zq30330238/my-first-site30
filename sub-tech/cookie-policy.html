Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners. Cookies help remember your preferences and enhance your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

At TechNest, we use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly. These enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We may use Google Analytics or similar services.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense and other advertising partners to deliver relevant ads based on your browsing history and interests. These cookies track your browsing across different websites.
  • Preference Cookies: Allow the website to remember choices you make (such as language preference) to provide a more personalized experience.

Google AdSense Cookies

Our website displays advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. Specifically:

  • Google uses the DoubleClick cookie to serve interest-based advertisements.
  • The DoubleClick cookie enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing history across the web.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of third-party cookies used for personalized advertising at www.aboutads.info.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to Google cookies, some of our third-party partners (such as analytics providers and social media platforms) may also place cookies on your device. We have no control over these third-party cookies.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites or all websites
  • Block third-party cookies specifically

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website and many others that you visit.

Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree, please adjust your browser settings or discontinue use of our website.

Updates to This Policy
