Story Arcs

The complete Tokyo Ghoul storyline, from Kaneki's first encounter with a ghoul to the epic conclusion of Tokyo Ghoul:re. Follow the journey through 179 chapters of the original manga and 179 chapters of the sequel.

Introduction Arc

Tokyo Ghoul Episodes 1-4

Ken Kaneki, a quiet college student, meets the beautiful Rize Kamishiro on a date. His life is shattered when Rize reveals herself as a ghoul and attacks him. Left for dead after a construction accident, Kaneki receives an organ transplant that turns him into a half-ghoul. He is taken in by the staff of Anteiku, a coffee shop run by ghouls who want to live peacefully among humans. Kaneki struggles to accept his new identity and the hunger that comes with it.

Key Events

Kaneki meets Rize, Organ transplant, Kaneki becomes half-ghoul, Meeting Touka and Yoshimura at Anteiku, Learning about ghoul society

Aogiri Arc

Tokyo Ghoul Episodes 5-9

Kaneki is kidnapped by Yakumo "Yamori" Omori of the Aogiri Tree organization. Subjected to brutal torture designed to break his spirit, Kaneki undergoes a profound psychological transformation. He accepts his ghoul nature and embraces the pain, emerging as a powerful and ruthless fighter. He defeats Yamori and begins to consolidate power, setting the stage for the conflict between the CCG, Aogiri Tree, and the peaceful ghouls of Anteiku.

Key Events

Kaneki's torture by Yamori, Kaneki's hair turns white, Kaneki accepts his ghoul nature, Defeat of Yamori, Kaneki joins Aogiri Tree's conflict

Anteiku Raid Arc

Tokyo Ghoul Episodes 10-12

The CCG launches a full-scale assault on Anteiku, believing it to be a ghoul hideout. Kaneki rushes to defend his friends while the CCG deploys their strongest investigators, including Kishou Arima. Yoshimura reveals his true identity as the original One-Eyed Owl and fights to protect his sanctuary. The battle results in the destruction of Anteiku and the apparent deaths of several key characters. Kaneki faces Arima in a devastating confrontation.

Key Events

CCG raids Anteiku, Yoshimura reveals his Owl form, Kaneki vs Arima, Destruction of Anteiku, Kaneki's apparent death

CCG Arc

Tokyo Ghoul:re Chapters 1-70

Three years after the Anteiku raid, a new CCG squad called the Quinx Squad is formed. The squad is led by Haise Sasaki — Kaneki with amnesia — who now works as a CCG investigator. The Quinx members, implanted with ghoul Kagune, hunt ghouls under Sasaki's guidance. As Sasaki investigates the mysterious Clown organization and Aogiri Tree's resurgence, fragments of his past as Kaneki begin to resurface.

Key Events

Formation of Quinx Squad, Haise Sasaki as leader, Introduction of :re coffee shop, Clown organization investigation, Sasaki's memories begin returning

:re Arc

Tokyo Ghoul:re Chapters 71-144

Sasaki fully recovers his memories as Kaneki and reconciles his dual identities. He takes control of the remnants of Aogiri Tree and establishes himself as the One-Eyed King, aiming to create a world where ghouls can live without persecution. The CCG and ghouls engage in an all-out war while dark secrets about the creation of ghouls are revealed. Kaneki's Dragon form emerges, threatening to destroy everything.

Key Events

Kaneki regains memories, Kaneki becomes One-Eyed King, Dragon Kaneki emergence, All-out war between CCG and ghouls, Revelations about ghoul origins

Final Arc

Tokyo Ghoul:re Chapters 145-179

In the final arc, Kaneki's Dragon form wreaks havoc on Tokyo, and the CCG must ally with ghouls to stop the catastrophe. The true nature of ghouls and the origin of RC cells are revealed. Kaneki's friends fight to save him from losing himself to the Dragon. The series concludes with a future where ghouls and humans begin the long path toward understanding and coexistence, with the next generation living in a more peaceful world.

Key Events

Dragon Kaneki rampage, Alliance between CCG and ghouls, Origin of ghouls revealed, Kaneki's rescue, Peaceful coexistence future
