CCG Organization

The Commission of Counter Ghoul (CCG) is the primary organization dedicated to investigating and eliminating ghoul threats. Based in Tokyo, the CCG employs trained investigators who wield Quinque — weapons crafted from ghoul Kagune — to combat dangerous ghouls.

About the CCG

Founded to protect human society from ghoul predation, the CCG operates a complex hierarchy ranging from Rank 1 investigators to the elite Special Class. Each investigator is trained in combat, investigation, and Quinque usage. The CCG also conducts research into ghoul biology and develops new anti-ghoul technologies.

The organization has multiple branches throughout Japan, with the main headquarters in Tokyo. The CCG's most significant advancement is the Quinx project, which surgically implants ghoul Kagune into humans to create investigators with ghoul-like abilities while maintaining their human willpower.

Key divisions include the S3 Squad (elite investigators), the Quinx Squad (Kagune-enhanced investigators), and the Research Division (ghoul biology and Quinque development).

Special Class

The Reaper

The strongest CCG investigator in history. Known as the Grim Reaper for his unparalleled combat record against S and SS-rated ghouls. Wields the Narukami Quinque.

Juuzou Suzuya

Special Class

The Reaper's Successor

A young Special Class investigator with a traumatic past. Uses twin Quinque Jason and 13's. Known for his unpredictable combat style and cheerful demeanor in battle.

Koutarou Amon

First Class

The Righteous

A principled investigator who wields the Exterminator Quinque. His moral journey from ghoul-hater to understanding the complexity of the ghoul-human conflict defines his arc.

Akira Mado

First Class

The Strategist

Daughter of the late Kureo Mado. A skilled investigator and tactical leader who values teamwork and analytical approaches to ghoul investigations.

Kuki Urie

Rank 1 (Quinx)

The Ambitious Leader

Leader of the Quinx Squad. An Ukaku-type Quinx who grows from an arrogant cadet to a mature leader capable of making difficult decisions.

Haise Sasaki

Rank 1 (Quinx)

The Amnesiac Mentor

The mentor of the Quinx Squad, later revealed to be Ken Kaneki with amnesia. A talented investigator with extraordinary combat instincts and deep empathy for his squad.
