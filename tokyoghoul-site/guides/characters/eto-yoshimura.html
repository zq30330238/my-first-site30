Eto Yoshimura

萊 都童

The One-Eyed Owl / Sen Takatsuki

Role

Antagonist / Leader

Affiliation

Aogiri Tree

Kagune

Ukaku / Rinkaku hybrid

RC Type

Hybrid

“This world is a cruel and beautiful place. And I intend to enjoy every moment of its suffering.”

Overview

Eto Yoshimura is a legendary figure in the ghoul world, known both as the SS-rated ghoul "The One-Eyed Owl" and as the bestselling author Sen Takatsuki. The daughter of Yoshimura and a human woman, she is a natural one-eyed ghoul. As the leader of Aogiri Tree, she orchestrates large-scale attacks on the CCG and manipulates events from the shadows.

Appearance

Eto appears as a petite young woman with long dark hair and a seemingly innocent face. As Sen Takatsuki, she wears elegant, gothic-inspired clothing. Her true form as the One-Eyed Owl is a massive, monstrous entity with multiple Kagune wings and a skeletal appearance. She can shift between her human and ghoul forms at will.

Personality

Eto is brilliant, manipulative, and deeply cynical. She views the world through a lens of nihilistic philosophy, believing that suffering reveals truth. As Sen Takatsuki, she is articulate and charming. Behind her intellectual facade lies a being who delights in chaos and destruction while pursuing a vision of ghoul liberation.

Abilities

Eto possesses both Ukaku and Rinkaku-type Kagune, making her a rare hybrid with immense power. Her Ukaku wings allow flight and long-range attacks, while her Rinkaku tentacles deliver devastating close-range damage. In her Owl form, she becomes nearly unstoppable, regenerating from severe injuries and overwhelming opponents with raw power.

Trivia

Eto writes novels under the pen name Sen Takatsuki, which Kaneki admires. Her books foreshadow major events in the series. She is the daughter of Yoshimura (the manager of Anteiku). Her character is inspired by classical literary figures. She is considered one of the most dangerous ghouls in existence.
