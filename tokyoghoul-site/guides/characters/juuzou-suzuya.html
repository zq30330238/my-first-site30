Overview

Juuzou Suzuya is one of the most formidable CCG investigators, rising to Special Class despite his traumatic past. Raised by the sadistic ghoul Big Madam, Juuzou was tortured and forced to act as a ghoul's plaything. After being rescued by Kishou Arima, he joined the CCG and became a uniquely effective investigator with an unorthodox fighting style.

Appearance

Juuzou is a slim young man with short white hair and a perpetual smile that masks his true emotions. He has visible scars from his childhood torture, including stitched wounds and missing body parts. He typically wears the standard CCG investigator uniform but accessorizes with bandages and has a childlike, almost androgynous appearance.

Personality

Juuzou appears cheerful, childlike, and often disturbingly carefree about violence. His traumatic upbringing left him with a fractured personality and unusual perception of pain and morality. He forms genuine bonds with his partners and shows surprising emotional depth. Despite his disturbing demeanor, he is fiercely loyal and develops a strong sense of justice.

Abilities

As a human, Juuzou relies on Quinque weapons crafted from ghoul Kagune. He wields multiple Quinque simultaneously with incredible skill, most notably the twin weapons Jason and 13's. His combat style is unpredictable and brutally efficient. He has superhuman agility and reflexes developed through years of survival.

Trivia

Juuzou's name is a pun in Japanese, with the characters suggesting "thirteenth" which appears as a motif. He was raised by Big Madam who forced him to call her "mother" while torturing him. His stitched smile is a result of being forced to smile constantly. He becomes a Special Class Investigator at an exceptionally young age. His Quinque names reference his past tormentors.
