Tatara

タタラ

The Flame Emperor

Role

Aogiri Tree Executive

Affiliation

Aogiri Tree

Kagune

Bikaku / Ukaku hybrid

RC Type

Hybrid

“Power is the only truth in this world. Everything else is just an illusion the weak use to comfort themselves.”

Overview

Tatara is a high-ranking executive of Aogiri Tree and one of its most feared members. Known for his brutal efficiency and enormous power, he commands operations across multiple wards. His Chinese origins and mysterious past add layers to his character. He serves as Eto's right hand and executes her most ambitious plans.

Appearance

Tatara has dark skin, long black hair, and a muscular build. He wears traditional Chinese-inspired clothing mixed with modern tactical gear. His face often bears a cruel smile, and his eyes glow with predatory intensity. His presence alone is enough to intimidate most opponents.

Personality

Tatara is cruel, ambitious, and utterly ruthless. He believes in survival of the fittest and respects only strength. He is fiercely loyal to Eto and Aogiri Tree's cause. Despite his brutality, he is intelligent and strategic, capable of long-term planning and manipulation.

Abilities

Tatara possesses a rare hybrid-type Kagune with both Bikaku and Ukaku traits. He can generate and control flames through his Kagune, giving him devastating ranged and melee capabilities. His combat style combines raw power with tactical precision. He is considered one of Aogiri's strongest fighters.

Trivia

Tatara's name and design draw inspiration from Chinese folklore. He is one of the few ghouls who can manipulate elements through their Kagune. His exact origins are kept mysterious throughout most of the series. He commands significant resources and influence within Aogiri Tree.
