Touka Kirishima

霧島 透香

The Rabbit / Anteiku Waitress

Role

Female Lead

Affiliation

Anteiku

Kagune

Ukaku

RC Type

Ukaku

“If you read, you'll learn things. If you learn things, you'll understand things. And if you understand things, the world becomes a more interesting place.”

Overview

Touka Kirishima is a young ghoul working as a waitress at Anteiku coffee shop. Known by her alias "The Rabbit" due to her rabbit-like Ukaku Kagune, she is fierce, independent, and protective of those she cares about. She becomes one of Kaneki's closest allies and guides him through the world of ghouls, helping him accept his new identity.

Appearance

Touka has short blue-black hair and piercing violet eyes. She typically wears the Anteiku waitress uniform or casual clothing suited for combat. When fighting, her Ukaku Kagune manifests as wing-like crystalline appendages from her shoulders, giving her an ethereal and dangerous appearance.

Personality

Touka is headstrong, impulsive, and fiercely protective. She has a sharp tongue but a deeply caring nature, especially toward her friends and brother. She struggles with her identity as a ghoul and fears being unable to protect those she loves. Her relationship with Kaneki helps her open up emotionally and learn to trust others.

Abilities

Touka has an Ukaku-type Kagune that manifests as crystalline wings capable of firing high-speed projectiles. Her Kagune is fast and powerful but has limited stamina. She is highly skilled in close-quarters combat and uses her speed and agility to overwhelm opponents. She develops advanced techniques including lightning-enhanced attacks.

Trivia

Touka's alias "Rabbit" comes from her distinctive Kagune shape. She loves reading and encourages Kaneki to read regularly. Her brother Ayato is a member of Aogiri Tree. She eventually opens her own coffee shop and renames it :re, continuing Anteiku's legacy. Her favorite coffee is a simple black brew.
