Kagune Types

In the world of Tokyo Ghoul, Kagune are the predatory organs of ghouls that manifest as weapons. They are classified into four distinct types based on their RC cell structure and manifestation patterns. Each type has unique strengths, weaknesses, and combat applications.

The four types are: Ukaku (feather-like), Koukaku (armor-like), Rinkaku (scale-like), and Bikaku (tail-like). A ghoul's Kagune type is determined by their RC cell type and is usually consistent throughout their life.

Ukaku ()

Ukaku-type Kagune erupt from the shoulders or upper back, forming wing-like crystalline structures. They excel in high-speed projectile attacks and aerial combat. Ukaku users are extremely fast but have limited stamina due to the high RC cell consumption of their Kagune.

Traits: High speed, long-range attacks, crystalline appearance, wing-like formation

Weakness: Low stamina, RC cell depletion with extended use, weaker in prolonged battles

Notable Users: Touka Kirishima, Eto Yoshimura, Kuki Urie, Kureo Mado, Yoshimura

Koukaku ()

Koukaku-type Kagune are the most solid and durable of all Kagune types. They manifest as hardened scales, shells, or blade-like protrusions from the user's body. Koukaku offers excellent defensive capabilities while also providing powerful melee attacks.

Traits: High defense, solid structure, blade-like formations, excellent durability

Weakness: Heavy and slow, limited mobility, requires high RC cell count to maintain

Notable Users: Hinami Fueguchi, Rize Kamishiro, Kurona Yasuhisa

Rinkaku ()

Rinkaku-type Kagune manifest as tentacle-like appendages from the lower back, typically crimson in color. They offer an excellent balance of offense and defense, with powerful striking capabilities and good regeneration. Rinkaku is the most common Kagune type among natural ghouls.

Traits: Tentacle-like appendages, excellent regeneration, balanced stats, crimson color

Weakness: Requires significant control, can be mentally unstable, visible manifestation

Notable Users: Ken Kaneki, Yakumo Omori, Seidou Takizawa, Riko, Noro

Bikaku ()

Bikaku-type Kagune extend from the tailbone area, forming tail-like appendages. They offer the best all-around balance of speed, power, and range. Bikaku users are versatile fighters capable of adapting to various combat situations.

Traits: Balanced stats, versatile combat, tail-like appearance, good range and speed

Weakness: No exceptional strengths, jack-of-all-trades but master of none

Notable Users: Shuu Tsukiyama, Tatara, Ayato Kirishima, Nishiki Nishio
